## 📝 摘要

A robotic surgical system is provided with rigid bending sleeves (416a) which extend through the same opening into the patient. A surgical instrument (500) having a passive flexible shaft (506) extends through these curved sleeves. A force isolating element (856) within the flexible shaft prevents the bending of the shaft from affecting the end effector actuating element that extends through the shaft. The sleeves are oriented to guide the instrument toward the surgical site. Various port members (1402) are disclosed in which the bushing is supported within the single opening (1902) for supporting the bushings during insertion into the single opening and mounting to the robotic manipulator. A remote operation control system is disclosed that moves these bending sleeves and their associated devices in a manner that allows the surgeon to experience intuitive control.

**（中文摘要）**

种机器人手术系统配置有(多个)刚性的弯曲套管(416a)，这些套管延伸穿过同个开口进入病人体内。具有(多个)被动柔性轴(506)的手术器械(500)延伸穿过这些弯曲套管。柔性轴内的力隔离元件(856)阻止轴的弯曲对延伸穿过轴的末端执行器致动元件的影响。这些套管被定向为朝手术部位引导器械。公开了在该单个开口内支撑弯曲套管的各种端口构件(1402)。公开了在插入到该单个开口以及安装至机器人操纵器上的过程中支撑这些套管的(多个)套管支撑固定件(1902)。公开了种远程操作控制系统，该系统以种允许外科医生体验直观控制的方式移动这些弯曲套管及其相关联的器械。
