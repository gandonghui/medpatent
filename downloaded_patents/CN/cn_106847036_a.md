## 📝 摘要

The invention provides a virtual reality technology-based artificial hip replacement teaching display system, which comprises a basic data memory unit, a surgical process and scene display unit, a virtual reality imaging unit and a surgery live broadcast unit, wherein the surgical process and scene display unit is connected with the basic data memory unit, the virtual reality imaging unit and the surgery live broadcast unit separately. According to the virtual reality technology-based artificial hip replacement teaching display system, a medical worker can have relatively intuitive experience on a surgery which needs multiple apparatus operations while experiencing the virtual reality technology; a medical technology can be better exercised and the learning exchange target can be achieved in the whole surgical simulation process; and procedures and specifications of artificial hip replacement can be more really learned and known.

**（中文摘要）**

本发明提供种基于虚拟现实技术的人工髋关节置换术教学展示系统，包括基础数据存储单元、手术流程及场景展示单元、虚拟现实成像单元、手术直播单元；所述手术流程及场景展示单元分别与基础数据存储单元、虚拟现实成像单元、手术直播单元连接。本发明提供的基于虚拟现实技术的人工髋关节置换术教学展示系统，使医护人员可以在体验到虚拟现实技术的同时对需要多种器械操作的手术有个比较直观的体验，在整个手术模拟过程当中可以让医疗技术得到更好的锻炼和达到学习交流目的，可以更加真实的学习和了解人工髋关节置换术的流程及规范。
