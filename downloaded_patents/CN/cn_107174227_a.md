## 📝 摘要

The invention provides a surgical anesthesia nursing device. The device comprises a monitoring display screen, a monitoring system, an internal control device, an epidural space anesthesia output port, a subarachnoid space anesthesia output port, an epidural space anesthesia input port, a subarachnoid space anesthesia input port, a monitoring control panel structure, an alarm device, a storage battery, a power socket, a blood pressure pump main engine, an indicator light and a handle; the monitoring display screen is connected to the outside of the internal control device through the monitoring system, and the epidural space anesthesia output port is electrically connected to the right side of the subarachnoid space anesthesia output port. Accordingly, by arranging the monitoring system, the internal control device, a sensor and the alarm device, operation is convenient, the monitoring efficiency is high, induction is sensitive, monitoring data display is accurate and intuitive, alarm monitoring is abnormal, the monitoring effect is further improved, safety and reliability are achieved, the device is practical and convenient, functional diversity is completed, and the best safe monitoring effect is achieved.

**（中文摘要）**

本发明提供种手术麻醉护理装置，包括监护显示屏，监护系统，内部控制装置，硬膜外腔麻醉输出孔，蛛网膜下腔麻醉输出孔，硬膜外腔麻醉输入孔，蛛网膜下腔麻醉输入孔，监护控制面板结构，报警装置，蓄电池，插电口，血压泵主机，指示灯和提手，所述的监护显示屏通过监护系统连接设置在内部控制装置的外部；所述的硬膜外腔麻醉输出孔通过电性连接设置在蛛网膜下腔麻醉输出孔的右侧。本发明的监护系统，内部控制装置，传感器和报警装置的设置，有利于操作方便，监护效率高，感应灵敏，使得监护数据显示准确直观，警报监护异常，进步使得提高监护效果，安全可靠，从而实用方便，完善功能多样性，达到最佳安全监护效果。
