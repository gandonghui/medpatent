## 📝 摘要

The invention discloses a sternum model device for a median sternotomy incision. The sternum model device comprises a left sternum flap, a right sternum flap and an elastic separating mechanism capable of separating the left sternum flap and the right sternum flap towards the two sides, wherein wire passing channels for allowing binding wires to pass through are reserved in the periphery of the left sternum flap and the periphery of the right sternum flap. The device comprises the left half sternum flap and the right half sternum flap which are longitudinally cut in the center. The left half sternum flap and the right half sternum flap are controlled by the elastic separating mechanism to be in the open state, and the state is similar to that the sternum split in the center is pulled to the two sides by the muscle group of the chest. The sternum model device is used for teaching demonstration of the thoracic and cardiovascular surgical operation; with the model device, the operation process is close to the operation process of annularly binding the sternum for closing the sternotomy incision by using steel wires in the real operation. The operation process can be intuitively conveyed for learning of students or teaching of teachers in medical schools, and thus the understanding of the students is facilitated.

**（中文摘要）**

本发明公开了种胸骨正中切口胸骨模型装置，包括左胸骨瓣、右胸骨瓣以及可将左胸骨瓣和右胸骨瓣向两侧分开的弹性分离机构，所述左胸骨瓣和右胸骨瓣外围留有供绑扎丝通过的走丝通道。该装置分为被正中纵向切开的左右两半胸骨瓣。左右两半胸骨瓣通过弹性分离机构控制并保持张开状态，与正中劈开的胸骨被胸部肌群向两侧牵拉相类似。本发明属于种用于胸心血管外科手术教学演示用的胸骨模型装置，利用该模型装置可更为接近真实手术中以钢丝环扎胸骨关闭胸骨切口的操作过程。使医学院学生学习或老师带教中更为直观传达手术过程，便于学生理解。
