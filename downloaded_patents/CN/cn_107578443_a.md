## 📝 摘要

The invention provides a needle knife display method and device. The invention provides a needle knife display method and device. The method comprises step 1, based on a CT sequence image and an MRI sequence image of a patient's diseased part with a plurality of marking points, acquiring a target three-dimensional structural body formed by the diseased tissue of the diseased part of the patient and surrounding sensitive tissues of the diseased part; step 2, registering the patient's diseased part with the plurality of marking points with the target three-dimensional structural body; step 3, acquiring the pose information of the tip of the needle knife, and displaying the three-dimensional image of the needle knife in the target three-dimensional structural body based on the pose information. According to the invention, the diseased tissue, the surrounding sensitive tissues, and the three-dimensional image of the needle knife can be displayed in the needle knife operation process in real time, so that a surgical operator can intuitively know the position relation of the tissues and the needle knife, so that the side effects such as complications, or irreversible damage, such as tendon fracture, nerve injury and the like, caused by insufficient experience or unfamiliarity with the anatomical structure can be prevented after the patient's operation.

**（中文摘要）**

本发明提供种针刀显示方法及装置。该方法包括：步骤1，基于具有若干标记点的患者病变部位CT序列图像和MRI序列图像，获取所述患者病变部位病变组织及其周围敏感组织构成的目标三维结构体；步骤2，将所述具有若干标记点的患者病变部位与所述目标三维结构体进行配准；步骤3，获取针刀刀尖的位姿信息，并基于所述位姿信息，在所述目标三维结构体中显示所述针刀的三维图像。本发明实现了在针刀手术过程中实时显示病变组织、其周围敏感组织和针刀的三维图像，以使手术实施者能够直观的了解它们的位置关系，避免因经验不足或对解剖结构不够熟悉导致患者手术后出现副作用，如并发症，或者不可逆的损伤，如肌腱断裂、神经损伤等。
