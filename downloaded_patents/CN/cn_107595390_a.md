## 📝 摘要

The invention provides a real-time matching fusion method for an ultrasonic image and a CT image. The method comprises the following steps: performing the ultrasonic scanning and CT scanning to a patient before an operation, and performing the matching fusion to each interest region and the corresponding CT image; in the operation, performing the image guidance surgical navigation through the ultrasonic scanning, to obtain a real-time ultrasonic image through the ultrasonic scanning, and to obtain a real-time matrix; and according to the arrangement position of each mark point in the real-timematrix, regulating the imaging angle of the CT image, and simultaneously displaying the ultrasonic image and the CT image on a display interface. The method is capable of realizing the matching fusion of the ultrasonic image and the CT image in the image guidance operation process, and displaying the corresponding ultrasonic image and CT image while the focus position is achieved, and the imagingangle of the CT image is regulated in real time according to the operation of a doctor. The intuitive corresponding ultrasonic image and CT image are conveniently obtained by the doctor, and the operating precision is improved.

**（中文摘要）**

本发明提出了种超声影像与CT影像的实时匹配融合方法，术前对患者进行超声扫描和CT扫描，将每个感兴趣区与对应CT影像进行匹配融合，术中，通过超声扫描进行图像引导手术导航，超声扫描获得实时超声影像，获得实时矩阵，根据各个标记点在实时矩阵中的排列位置，调整CT影像的成像角度，超声影像与CT影像同时呈现在显示界面上。本发明实现了图像引导手术过程中超声影像与CT影像的匹配融合，到达病灶位置时，呈现彼此对应的超声影像与CT影像，而且CT影像的成像角度根据医生的操作进行实时调整，方便医生得到直观的、彼此对应的超声影像与CT影像，提高手术的精确度。
