## 📝 摘要

The invention discloses a weight calculation multifunctional surgical tray. The weight calculation multifunctional surgical tray comprises a main body and a tray body; the right of the main body is fixedly provided with a support rod, the surface of the support rod is provided with a movable clamping ring in a sleeve mode, an adjusting knob penetrates through the movable clamping ring, and the left end of the movable clamping ring is welded with a base; the bottom surface of the tray body is connected with a weight meter, the edge of the tray body is welded with a baffle, the upper surface ofthe tray body is fixedly provided with a storage case, and the inside of the storage case is provided with a storage drawer in a penetrating mode, the front surface of the storage drawer is fixedly provided with a handle, the left of the storage case is fixedly provided with a transparent box, and the upper end of the bottom surface of the tray body is provided with a magnetic plate. The weight calculation multifunctional surgical tray can accurately calculate the weight of instruments inside the tray body, perform detection through the weight meter and then display the weight through a weightdisplay unit, thereby being intuitive, clear, convenient, rapid and applicable to production and application and having a good development prospect.

本发明公开了种具有重量计算的多功能手术托盘，包括主体与托盘，主体的右侧固定有支撑杆，支撑杆的表面套合有活动卡环，活动卡环的内部穿插有调节旋钮，且活动卡环的左端焊接有底座，托盘的底面连接有计重器，托盘的边沿焊接有挡板，且托盘的上表面固定有收纳箱，收纳箱的内部穿插有收纳抽屉，收纳抽屉的正表面固定有拉手，收纳箱的左侧固定有透明盒，托盘的底面上端连接有磁性板。该种具有重量计算的多功能手术托盘可对托盘内部的器械重量做出准确计重，通过计重器做出检测，再通过重量显示器显示出来，直观明了，方便快捷，适用于具有重量计算的多功能手术托盘的生产和使用，具有良好的发展前景。
