## 📝 摘要

The present invention discloses a dual-purpose surgery simulation training device. The device comprises a rotation operation unit, a surgical operation portion is fixedly installed on the rotation operation unit, the rotation operation unit and the surgical operation portion are both provided with information feedback units configured to read rotation angles or displacement signals and drive unitsconfigured to provide feedback force, the surgical operation portion is provided with a pressure induction unit, and the pressure induction unit is configured to detect a force on the surgical operation portion. Through arrangement of the pressure induction unit, the dual-purpose surgery simulation training device achieves dual functions of surgery simulation and man-made model operation by an operator, the pressure induction unit is configured to monitor the force when the surgical operation portion touches a human organ, the force concrete value is transmitted to a computer screen, the value is displayed and an upper limit value is arranged to train the operator's hand feeling to allow the operator's operation feeling to be more intuitive.

**（中文摘要）**

本发明公开了种两用手术仿真训练装置，包括转动操作单元，转动操作单元上固定安装有手术操作部，转动操作单元与手术操作部上均安装有可读取其转动角度或位移信号的信息反馈单元以及提供反馈力的驱动单元，手术操作部上安装有压力感应单元，压力感应单元用以检测手术操作部受到的力。本发明通过压力感应单元的设置实现了虚拟手术的模拟和操作者对人造模型操作的双重功能；压力感应单元用于监测手术操作部碰到人体器官时受到的力，并将受力的具体数值传输与计算机屏幕上，通过数值的显示和上限值的设置训练操作者的手感，使操作者的操作感受更直观。
