## 📝 摘要

The invention provides a novel minimally invasive surgical instrument control device based on flexible wire transmission, which comprises a fixing component (1), a drivingcomponent (2), a mechanical arm component (3) and an end actuator (4).The fixing component comprises a wrist support and a shell; the driving component comprises a hand-held frame, a connecting belt, a driving pulley, a reversingroller, a cable and the like; the mechanical arm component comprises a bent rod and a straight rod; the end actuator includes a joint, a spring, etc. A doctor drives the driving pulley to rotate by holding the hand-held frame, so that the wound cable is subjected to displacement change, so that the end actuator generates corresponding response to the instruction sent by the doctor. According to the minimally invasive surgical instrument, the arm and the wrist-shaped control can be realized easily and intuitively, the rotation of any angle in the human body can be realized, the structure is simple and unique, the cost is low, the practicability is strong, the working efficiency is high, and the requirements of the surgical doctor on the minimally invasive surgical instrument can be met.

**（中文摘要）**

本发明提供了种基于柔性丝传动的新型微创手术器械控制装置，基于柔性丝传动的新型微创手术器械控制装置包括固定组件(1)、驱动组件(2)、机械臂组件(3)和末端执行器(4)。固定组件包括腕托、外壳；驱动组件包括手持架、连接带、驱动滑轮、换向滚轮、线缆等；机械臂组件包括弯杆、直杆；末端执行器包括关节、弹簧等。医生通过手握手持架带动驱动滑轮旋转，使缠绕在上面的线缆跟着产生位移变化，进而使末端执行器对医生发出的指令产生相应的响应。本发明能灵巧和直观的实现手臂、腕状控制，能实现人体内任意角度的转动，并且结构简单独特、成本低廉，实用性强，工作效率高，能够满足手术医生对微创手术器械的要求。
