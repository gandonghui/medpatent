## 📝 摘要

The invention relates to the field of medical equipment, in particular to an anesthetic device used for lidocaine anesthetics. The anesthetic device used for the lidocaine anesthetic comprises a smearing structure, a storage cylinder, a detection structure, a limiting structure, a driving structure, a measuring structure, a sealing sleeve and a sealing ring. The storage cylinder is used for storing the anesthetics, the driving structure is used for driving anesthetic for extrusion, and the smearing structure is used for uniformly smearing a surgical spot. The detection structure is used for conveniently detecting the anesthetic effect, the measuring structure is used for conveniently and intuitively measuring anesthetic liquid level inside the storage cylinder, the limiting structure is used for improving the stability. The sealing sleeve is used for convenient protection, and the sealing rings used for improving the sealing performance. The smearing structure is matched with the storage cylinder to enable the anesthetics to be smeared on the undamaged skin surface of surgical spot to be operated, the surgical spot is massaged gently by the smearing structure, the absorption of anesthetics in the surgical spot is improved, the anesthetic effect is improved, the detection structure is slidably connected with the storage cylinder, so that the anesthesia effect of the anesthesia site is directly detected by the detection structure.
