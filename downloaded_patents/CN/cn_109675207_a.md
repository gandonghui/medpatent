## 📝 摘要

The invention discloses a surgical guide plate for back-mounted radiotherapy of cervical carcinoma. The surgical guide plate comprises an internal substrate fit with an uterine neck, wherein a sourceapplicator sleeve hole is formed in the center of the internal substrate, and inserting needle through holes I are formed in the periphery of the source applicator sleeve hole; an outward-expanding ring plate is fixed at the outer side of the internal substrate in a sleeve manner, and inserting needle through holes II are formed in the outward-expanding ring plate. According to the surgical guideplate for the back-mounted radiotherapy of the cervical carcinoma, doctors can intuitively determine the insertion depth of the source applicator or inserting needles so as to be applicable to patients of different vagina lengths. The guide plate can be rotated according to different positions of tumors, so that needle passages can be located at tumor target positions as much as possible, and unnecessary needle passages are reduced. Big and small sizes are available and can meet the requirements of patients of different body dimensions. In addition, a guide plate with relatively large needle passage spacing can have a sealing device, and thus, the sloshing of the source applicator or the inserting needles can be effectively reduced. The surgical guide plate can be used for assisting clinicians to reasonably arrange the inserting needles and the source applicator, so that the dosage planning is more accurate, and the accuracy and reliability of surgical operations can be greatly improved.
