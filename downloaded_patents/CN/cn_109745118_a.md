## 📝 摘要

The invention relates to a therapeutic instrument. The therapeutic instrument comprises a center control displayer, a metal detector, a camera, a signal indicator, a special surgical instrument, an ultrasonic instrument and an ultrasonic probe which is connected with the ultrasonic instrument, wherein the surgical instrument, the signal indicator, the metal detector and the camera are connected insequence, and the center control displayer is connected with the camera and the ultrasonic instrument. It is achieved that the camera and the metal detector monitor the surgical instrument in the process of treating the human sensitive system, such as blood vessels and nerves; through the center control displayer and the signal indicator, surgical medical operation is intuitively and accurately conducted in a visible and indicative state in time to complete surgical treatment, subjective judgments of human factors are reduced, and the risk of original surgery is reduced. The surgical successrate and the surgical treatment rate are greatly improved, and the aim of relieving the pain of a patient is achieved.

**（中文摘要）**

本发明涉及一种治疗仪，通过中控显示器、金属探测器、摄像头、信号指示器、专用手术器具、超声仪、与超声仪相连接的超声探头；手术器具、信号指示器、金属探测器、摄像头依次相连接，中控显示器分别与摄像头和超声仪相连接；实现了摄像头和金属探测器对手术器具在治疗人体血管、神经的敏感系统过程中的监测，通过中控显示器和信号指示器将手术医疗操作在可视、指示的状态下直观、准确及时的完成手术治疗，降低了人为因素的主观判断同时降低了原有手术的风险，极大的提高了手术的成功率和治疗率，并达到了减轻病人痛苦的目的。
