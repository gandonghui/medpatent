## 📝 摘要

The invention discloses a steel wire traction tool for orthopaedics and a method. The steel wire traction tool comprises a moulding machine and a guide wire, wherein the moulding machine comprises a main body and a handle; the main body is arc-shaped high-hardness steel products; a first through hole is formed in the main body; one end of the main body is connected with the handle, and an openingis formed in the other end of the main body; one end of the handle is in the shape of a column, and the other end of the handle is in the shape of a quadrilateral; a second through hole is formed in the handle; the main body is connected to the quadrilateral; the first through hole and the second through hole are consistent in size, and are used for enabling the guide wire to enter from the top ofthe handle and penetrate out from the opening of the main body; the guide wire consists of a needle body and a cylinder; the cylinder is located at one end of the needle body and the diameter of thecylinder is greater than that of the needle body; and a third through hole is formed in the other end of the needle body, and is used for enabling the steel wire to penetrate. Through the adoption ofthe steel wire traction tool disclosed by the invention, trauma of surgical instruments to human body tissue and damage to nerves in the surgery process are effectively reduced, so that bleeding volume is reduced; in the surgery process, a doctor can be more convenient and swifter, and the operation can be more flexible; and in the surgery process, the doctor can intuitively know which length of the steel wire needs to be selected.

**（中文摘要）**

本发明公开了一种骨科用钢丝牵引工具和方法，包括塑形器和导丝，所述塑形器包括主体和把手，主体是呈弧形的高硬度钢材，内部设置有第一通孔，主体一端连接于把手，另一端设有开口；把手一端呈柱形，另一端为四边体，内部设置有第二通孔；主体连接于四边体上，第一通孔和第二通孔大小一致，用于使导丝从把手顶部进入再从主体开口处穿出；所述导丝由针体和柱体构成，所述柱体位于针体一端且直径大于针体，针体另一端设置有第三通孔，用于穿过钢丝；本发明有效减少了手术过程中手术器械对人体组织的创伤和对神经的损伤，减少了失血量；使医生在手术过程中能更加方便快捷。也更灵活；使医生在手术过程中能直观的知道需要选用哪种长度的钢丝。
