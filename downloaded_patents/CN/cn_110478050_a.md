## 📝 摘要

The invention discloses a method for fusion of a three-dimensional image and an endoscopic image based on CT/MRI data, including the following steps: acquiring a three-dimensional model established according to CT or MRI scanning data; carrying out real-time acquisition of an endoscopic surgical image; carrying out real-time acquisition of a three-dimensional model image which is dynamically synchronized with the endoscopic surgical image; and using the endoscopic surgical image as a background to make the three-dimensional model image overlap the position of the endoscopic surgical image. Theinvention also discloses a device and a system with application of the above endoscopic image fusion method. By overlapping and dynamically synchronizing the three-dimensional model image and the endoscopic surgical image, guided positioning of the endoscopic surgery can be intuitively performed to achieve fast and precise surgical requirements, accelerate the speed of surgery and reduce the riskof surgery. The method of the invention is suitable for promotion and application.

**（中文摘要）**

本发明公开了一种基于CT/MRI数据的三维图像与内镜影像融合方法,包括：获取根据CT或MRI扫描数据建立的三维模型；实时获取内镜的手术影像；实时获取与所述内镜的手术影像动态同步的三维模型图像；将所述内镜的手术影像作为背景，使所述三维模型图像与内镜的手术影像位置重合。本发明还公开了一种内置上述内镜影像融合方法的装置、系统。本发明通过将三维模型图像与内镜的手术影像位置重合、动态同步，可以直观地对内镜手术进行引导定位，达到快速精准的手术要求，加快了手术的速度，减少了手术的风险，适于推广应用。
