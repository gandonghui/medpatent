## 📝 摘要

The invention provides a fabrication method of an experiment model for tissue imaging at different depths. The fabrication method comprises the steps of cutting fresh chicken breast block to a cuboidmeat loaf, mixing a to-be-detected material and agar glue according to a volume ratio being 1:1 so as to fix a cake-shaped to-be-detected material simulation body, separating muscle fibers of the meatloaf at different depths by blunt surgical pliers, and filling each layer with the to-be-detected material simulation body; and respectively covering a left surface and a right surface of the cuboidmeat loaf with transparent plastic cover glass pieces consistent with areas of a left side surface and a right side surface of the meat loaf so as to achieve a support effect, wherein the whole cuboidmeat loaf is supported from the bottom by a support rack. By the fabrication method, the material can be simply and effectively fixed in muscle fiber gaps at different thicknesses, the position of the material is intuitively observed, repeated and blind scanning operation is prevented, so that the lifetime of laser is prolonged, the experiment efficiency is improved, the chicken tissue used by adie is uniform in background signal, and a favorable reference can be provided for research of a signal-to-noise ratio.

**（中文摘要）**

本发明提供一种不同深度组织成像实验模型的制备方法，将新鲜鸡胸肉块切成长方体肉块，将待测材料与agar胶按照体积比1：1混合固定成圆饼状的待测材料仿体，用钝性手术镊将不同深度处的肉块的肌纤维分离开，每层内均塞入所述待测材料仿体，然后，采用与所述肉块左右两侧面面积一致的透明的塑料盖玻片分别覆盖所述长方体肉块的左右两面以起支撑作用，整个长方体肉块被一支撑架从底部支撑。本发明能简单有效地将材料固定在不同厚度的肌纤维间隙，直观地观察到材料的位置而避免反复、盲目地进行扫描操作，从而节约激光寿命，提高实验效率，且模具所用鸡肉组织背景信号均一，可为信噪比的研究提供良好参考。
