## 📝 摘要

The invention relates to a navigation registration system, in particular to a titanium nail registration device for an artificial cochlea navigation operation and a method thereof. The titanium nail registration device for the artificial cochlea navigation operation is characterized in that the head of a patient is fixed on a head frame, special optical marking points are fixed on the head frame and a tail end operation tool, and four titanium nails are implanted on the rear side of the auricle before the operation so as to be matched with a probe. The invention also provides the titanium nailregistration method for the artificial cochlea navigation operation, which comprises the following steps of: scanning a head CT before the operation, and reconstructing key parts such as the titaniumnail, a cochlea and the like in a three-dimensional manner; taking the head frame as a reference system, sequentially pointing the probes to the titanium nails, and acquiring coordinates of the probes and mark points on the head frame through a binocular tracking sensor; calculating a coordinate transformation relationship between the titanium nail and the head frame to realize registration froman image space to an operation space; and reading the position and posture of the optical marking point on a surgical tool in real time to realize navigation. According to the device and the method, the operation is more intuitive, the operation is convenient, the operation precision is improved, and the operation risk is reduced.

**（中文摘要）**

本发明涉及导航配准系统，尤其涉及一种用于人工耳蜗导航手术的钛钉配准装置与方法。一种用于人工耳蜗导航手术的钛钉配准装置，将患者头部固定于头架，头架和末端手术工具上固定有特殊光学标记点，术前在耳廓后侧植入4根钛钉，以供探针配合。本发明同时提供一种用于人工耳蜗导航手术的钛钉配准方法，术前扫描头部CT，三维重建出钛钉和耳蜗等关键部位；将头架作为参照系，将探针依次指到各个钛钉上，通过双目跟踪传感器获取探针和头架上的标记点坐标；计算出钛钉与头架的坐标转换关系，实现从影像空间到手术空间的配准；实时读取手术工具上的光学标记点位姿，实现导航。该装置与方法使手术更加直观，便于操作，提高了手术精度，降低了手术风险。
