## 📝 摘要

The invention discloses a device for separate storage and disinfection of special-purpose surgical instruments. The device includes a box body, wherein three lifting slots with upward openings are evenly formed in the front and back of the top wall of the box body, a revolving door shaft is fixedly arranged between the front end wall and rear end wall of the top of the lifting slot, and torsion springs are connected among the revolving door shaft and the front end wall and rear end wall of the lifting slot; the torsion springs are rotatably connected to a revolving door, a wiping mechanism isarranged around the lifting slot, and a sixth slide slot is arranged on the bottom wall of the lifting slot; a spraying mechanism which is located on the left of the lifting slot is arranged above thesixth slide slot, a glass display plate is fixedly arranged on the right end wall of the sixth slide slot, and a placement mechanism is arranged in the sixth slide slot; and pulling rod mechanisms are symmetrically arranged at the front and backs of the sixth slide slot and the lifting slot, a spline shaft is arranged at the left end wall of the sixth slide slot, and the spline shaft is rotatablyconnected with the box body. Different special-purpose surgical instruments can be stored in separate grids, and can be taken more conveniently and intuitively, placement of the special-purpose surgical instruments is also more convenient and organized, and storage, wiping and disinfection can be performed at the same time.

**（中文摘要）**

本发明公开了一种专用手术器械分格存取消毒装置，包括箱体，箱体顶壁前后均匀设置有三个开口向上的升降槽，升降槽顶端前后端壁之间固定设置有转门轴，转门轴与升降槽前后端壁之间通过扭簧连接，扭簧与转门转动连接，升降槽周围设置有擦拭机构，升降槽底壁设置有第六滑槽，第六滑槽上方设置有位于升降槽左侧的喷洒机构，第六滑槽右端壁固定设置有玻璃展示板，第六滑槽内设置有放置机构，第六滑槽与升降槽前后对称设置有拉绳机构，第六滑槽左端壁设置有花键轴，花键轴与箱体转动连接，可以将不同的专用手术器械分格安置起来，拿取更加方便直观，专用手术器械的安置也更加方便有条理，而且可以在存取同时擦拭消毒。
