## 📝 摘要

The invention relates to an uncalibrated object registration method for a preoperative three-dimensional image and an intraoperative perspective image. The uncalibrated object registration method comprises the following steps: 1), establishing an anatomical and frequency-related system noise model M for simulating reality; 2), rendering CT/MRI data to generate three-dimensional volume data, addinganatomical noise and frequency noise to the DRR image to generate vivid image data similar to an X-ray film, and extracting a skeleton contour; 3), in an operation, enabling an X-ray machine to acquire two X-Ray perspective images at different angles; 4), generating N DRR images according to operation requirements to obtain pose coordinates P of each image; 5), processing the image data generatedby the DRR algorithm, then selecting a region of interest without a fracture part, and extracting a contour; 6), extracting the contour line of the real X-Ray according to the same method; and 7), accurately registering the preoperative three-dimensional image and the X-Ray perspective image based on the related properties of the contour line and the feature points, and outputting the accuratelyregistered image. According to the method, the registration precision can be improved, and the surgical navigation operation is more intuitive.

**（中文摘要）**

本发明涉及一种术前三维影像与术中透视图像的无标定物配准方法，包括以下步骤：1)建立一个模拟现实的解剖学和频率相关的系统噪声模型M；2)将CT/MRI数据渲染生成三维体数据，通过为DRR图像添加解剖噪声和频率噪声，生成比较逼真的类似于X光片的影像数据，提取骨骼轮廓；3)手术中，X射线机获取不同角度的两张X‑Ray透视图像；4)根据手术需求生成N个DRR图像，得到每个图像的位姿坐标P；5)将DRR算法生成的影像数据进行处理，然后选择没有骨折部位的感兴趣区域，提取轮廓；6)按同样方法提取真实X‑Ray的轮廓线；7)基于轮廓线和特征点相关的性质为术前三维影像和X‑Ray透视图像精确配准，输出精确配准后的图像。本方法能提高配准精度，使手术导航操作更直观。
