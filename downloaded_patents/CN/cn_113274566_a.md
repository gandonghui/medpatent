## 📝 摘要

The invention relates to a negative-pressure drainage device and a negative-pressure drainage method. The negative-pressure drainage device comprises a vessel, wherein the vessel has an internal cavity and a drainage port communicating with the internal cavity, and at least three mutual-partitioned metering chambers are formed in the internal cavity. The negative-pressure drainage device further comprises a regulating mechanism and a communicating mechanism arranged in the internal cavity, wherein the communicating mechanism comprises an inlet communicating with the drainage port and at least three outlets communicating with the inlet, and the regulating mechanism is used for controlling communication or not between the outlets and corresponding metering chambers. The negative-pressure drainage device is simple and compact in structure and reasonable in design, nurses can directly read postoperative daily drainage volume of a sufferer conveniently, the problem of accumulative error in the prior art can be effectively eliminated, doctors can intuitively look over the postoperative daily drainage volume of the sufferer and the variation trend of the drainage volume conveniently, and thus, the doctors can more conveniently evaluate the surgical operation conditions and postoperative recovery conditions of the sufferer.

**（中文摘要）**

本发明涉及一种负压引流装置及负压引流方法，包括容器，所述容器构造有内部空腔及与所述内部空腔相连通的引流口，所述内部空腔中构造有至少三个相互分隔的计量腔，还包括调节机构及设置于所述内部空腔中的连通机构，所述连通机构包括与所述引流口相连通的进口和至少三个与所述进口相连通的出口，所述调节机构用于控制出口与所对应的计量腔是/否连通；本负压引流装置，结构简单、紧凑，设计合理，不仅便于护士直接读取患者术后每天的引流量，并可以有效消除现有技术所存在的累计误差问题，而且便于医生直观的查看患者术后每天的引流量及引流量的变化趋势，从而更便于医生评估患者的手术情况及术后的恢复情况。
