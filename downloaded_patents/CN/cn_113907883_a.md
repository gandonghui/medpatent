## 📝 摘要

The invention discloses an ear side skull base surgery 3D visual surgical navigation system and method, and the system comprises an image obtaining device which is used for obtaining a two-dimensional scanning image of a lesion region of a patient before an operation; a three-dimensional scanner which is used for acquiring contour data of a lesion part of a patient before an operation; a processing device which is in communication connection with the image acquisition device and the three-dimensional scanner, constructs a three-dimensional model and an outline model according to the two-dimensional scanning image and the outline data, and superposes the three-dimensional model and the outline model to form a preoperative three-dimensional combined model; a contour model is constructed according to the contour data, so that the contour model is closer to a real person, the accuracy of surgical navigation is improved, and a doctor can intuitively obtain the position, size, property and other related information of a lesion through the preoperative three-dimensional combination model, so that the doctor can determine a surgical scheme conveniently.

**（中文摘要）**

本发明公开了一种耳侧颅底外科3D可视化手术导航系统及方法，其中耳侧颅底外科3D可视化手术导航系统，包括：图像获取装置，用于获取病人手术前的病变区的二维扫描图像；三维扫描仪，用于获取病人手术前的病变所处部位的外形轮廓数据；处理装置，其与所述图像获取装置所述三维扫描仪通讯连接，根据所述二维扫描图像和所述外形轮廓数据分别构建三维立体模型和外形轮廓模型，并将所述三维立体模型与所述外形轮廓模型叠加形成术前三维组合模型，根据外形轮廓数据构建外形轮廓模型，使得外形轮廓模型更贴近于真人，有利于提高手术导航的精确性，医生通过术前三维组合模型可直观地获取病变的位置、大小、性质等相关信息，从而便于医生确定手术的方案。
