## 📝 摘要

The objective of the present invention is to provide a surgical instrument for allowing the motion of bending a real end tool or performing surgery to intuitively correspond with the operation of a corresponding manipulation part. More specifically, to this end, provided are: the end tool having a plurality of degrees of freedom; the manipulation part having a structure capable of intuitively manipulating the motion of the end tool; and a power transmission part for transmitting the driving force of the manipulation part to the end tool so that the end tool can be moved according to the manipulation of the manipulation part.

**（中文摘要）**

本发明的目的在于提供一种手术用机械，其用于使实际末端工具曲折或执行手术的动作与相应的操作部运转直观上一致。具体地，出于上述目的，提供：末端工具，其具有复数个自由度；操作部，其具有能够直观地操作末端工具的结构；动力传递部，其将操作部的驱动力传递至末端工具以使末端工具根据操作部的操作进行动作。
