## 📝 摘要

The invention provides a thoracic surgery endoscope surgical instrument taking and storing device applied to the field of medical instruments. The thoracic surgery endoscope surgical instrument taking and storing device comprises a storage main body, a plurality of storage boxes, a plurality of detecting mechanisms and a control module. The storage main body is constructed to be provided with a plurality of storage cavities; the storage boxes are configured to be used for placing surgical instruments, the storage boxes are in one-to-one correspondence with the storage cavities, and the storage boxes are movably connected with the storage cavities and extend to the outside through the openings; the detection mechanism is constructed to be used for detecting the type and the health degree of the surgical instrument in the storage box; and the control module is configured to be in communication connection with the detection mechanism. According to the surgical instrument taking and storing device, surgical instruments can be placed in order, and the surgical instruments can be well stored when not used; meanwhile, the types and health degrees of the surgical instruments in the storage box are detected through the detection module, so that more intuitive experience is provided for the user, and waste of surgical time caused by selection of improper surgical instruments is avoided.

**（中文摘要）**

本发明提供了应用于医疗器械领域的一种胸外科腔镜手术器械拿取收纳装置，包括收纳主体、多个收纳盒、多个检测机构以及控制模块；收纳主体，被构造成具有多个收纳腔；收纳盒，被构造成用于放置手术器械，且收纳盒与收纳腔一一对应，收纳盒与收纳腔活动连接并通过开口伸出到外界；检测机构，被构造用于检测收纳盒中的手术器械的类型以及健康程度；控制模块，被构造成与检测机构通讯连接。本申请通过使用本申请的拿取收纳装置，能够使得手术器械的摆放具有条理性，以及在不使用时能够很好的被保存；同时通过检测模块检测收纳盒中的手术器械的类型以及健康程度，从而给予使用者更直观的体验，避免选择不恰当的手术器械而导致浪费手术时间。
