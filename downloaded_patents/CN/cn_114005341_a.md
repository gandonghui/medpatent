## 📝 摘要

The invention relates to an orthognathic surgery teaching mold and a use method thereof, the orthognathic surgery teaching mold comprises an upper jaw mold and a lower jaw mold, the upper jaw mold comprises an upper jaw bone module and an upper dentition module, and the upper dentition module is detachably connected with the upper jaw bone module; the lower jaw mold comprises a lower jawbone module and a lower dentition module, and the lower dentition module is detachably connected with the lower jawbone module; particularly, the upper dentition module is connected with the upper jawbone module in an inserted mode or in a magnetic attraction mode, and the lower dentition module is connected with the lower jawbone module in an inserted mode or in a magnetic attraction mode. The invention provides a surgical operation mold for medical students or doctors, so that the medical students and the doctors can more vividly and intuitively understand the anatomical structure of the jaw bone, the bone cutting part of the orthognathic surgery, the moving direction and the moving effect after the jaw bone is cut open, the firm internal fixing part of the jaw bone and the operation method, and the like, and the deep understanding of the medical students and the doctors on the orthognathic surgery can be improved, the training period of orthognathic surgery specialists is shortened, and more qualified orthognathic surgery specialists can be cultivated.

**（中文摘要）**

本发明涉及一套正颌外科手术教学模具及其使用方法，包括上颌模具和下颌模具，上颌模具包括上颌骨模块和上牙列模块，上牙列模块与所述上颌骨模块可拆卸连接；下颌模具，包括下颌骨模块和下牙列模块，所述下牙列模块与下颌骨模块可拆卸连接；特别的，所述上牙列模块与所述上颌骨模块插接或磁吸连接，所述下牙列模块与下颌骨模块插接或磁吸连接。本申请为医学生或医生提供了手术操作模具，可以让医学生和医生更生动和直观地了解颌骨的解剖结构、正颌手术的切骨部位、颌骨切开后的移动方向及移动效果、颌骨坚固内固定的部分和操作方法等，可提高其对正颌手术的深刻理解，缩的短正颌外科专科医生的培训周期，利于培养更多更合格的正颌外科专科医生。
