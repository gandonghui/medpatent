## 📝 摘要

The invention relates to the technical field of dental medical instruments, in particular to an implant handpiece, a dental implant surgical robot and an implant handpiece head adjusting method. A planting mobile phone can be driven by a driving device and comprises a planting mobile phone body and a planting mobile phone head arranged at one end of the planting mobile phone body and further comprises an adjusting device arranged between the planting mobile phone body and the planting mobile phone head. The adjusting device is configured to be used for adjusting the included angle between the planting mobile phone body and the planting mobile phone head. By arranging the adjusting device between the planting handpiece body and the planting handpiece head, the angle adjusting flexibility of the planting handpiece head is greatly improved compared with the prior art, and the planting handpiece head can fully adapt to different operation postures and punching angle requirements. Moreover, as the adjusting device is arranged at the head of the planting handpiece, the angle change of the head of the planting handpiece is more intuitive than the operation scheme of a swing arm in the prior art, and more conforms to the operation intuition of a user.

本发明涉及牙科医疗器械技术领域，特别是涉及种植手机及牙科种植手术机器人及种植手机机头调节方法。一种种植手机，能够被驱动装置驱动，包括种植手机机身和设置在种植手机机身一端的种植手机机头，种植手机还包括设置在种植手机机身与种植手机机头之间的调节装置，调节装置被配置为用于调节种植手机机身和种植手机机头之间的夹角。通过在种植手机机身与种植手机机头之间设置调节装置，种植手机机头的角度调节灵活程度相比现有技术得到大幅提高，能够充分适应不同的操作姿态和打孔角度需求。不仅如此，由于调节装置设置在种植手机机头处，这就使得种植手机机头的角度变化相比现有技术中摆臂的操作方案更为直观，更加符合用户的操作直觉。
