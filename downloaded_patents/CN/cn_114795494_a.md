## 📝 摘要

The invention provides an auxiliary device and a surgical system.The auxiliary device comprises a head-mounted device and a controller, and an endoscope installed at the tail end of a mechanical arm of a surgical robot and the head-mounted device are both in communication connection with the controller; the head-mounted equipment comprises a first display unit and a first camera shooting unit; the first camera shooting unit is configured to collect an in-vitro operation scene image of a patient; the controller is configured to send one of the patient in-vivo operation scene image and the patient in-vitro operation scene image corresponding to a scene switching instruction to the first display unit for display according to the scene switching instruction sent by a user. The auxiliary device provided by the invention can provide a clear and intuitive surgery scene image in the body of a patient for a user (nurse), so that the user (nurse) can carry out auxiliary operations such as liquid suction and the like in the surgery process more conveniently.

**（中文摘要）**

本发明提供了一种辅助装置以及手术系统，所述辅助装置包括头戴式设备和控制器，安装于手术机器人的机械臂的末端的内窥镜和头戴式设备均与控制器通信连接；头戴式设备包括第一显示单元和第一摄像单元；第一摄像单元配置为采集患者体外手术场景图像；控制器配置为根据使用者发送的场景切换指令，将患者体内手术场景图像和患者体外手术场景图像中的与场景切换指令相对应的一者发送至第一显示单元进行显示。本发明提供的辅助装置可以为使用者(护士)提供清晰、直观的患者体内手术场景图像，从而可以更加便于使用者(护士)在手术过程中进行吸液等辅助操作。
