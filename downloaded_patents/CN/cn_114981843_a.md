## 📝 摘要

Systems and methods for improved intraoperative microCT imaging of explant tissue samples and for improved visualization of such samples are provided. These embodiments provide reduced scan time and the ability of radiologists to quickly receive useful scan portraits and provide accurately communicated suggestions to surgical surgeons. An improved scan visualization approach facilitates surgeons and radiologists to interact with scan data, including annotation, observation, and re-orientation, to accurately reflect the orientation of an imaged tissue sample relative to the body prior to explant. The improved visualization methods include color-coded sample textures indicative of the orientation of the sample, color-coded tumor visualization indicative of proximity to the edges of the sample, and intuitive methods for adjusting the position and orientation of the two-dimensional visualization relative to the sample.

**（中文摘要）**

提供了用于外植组织样本的改进的术中显微CT成像和用于这种样本的改进的可视化的系统和方法。这些实施例提供了减少的扫描时间以及放射科医师快速接收有用的扫描画像并向手术外科医生提供准确传达的建议的能力。改进的扫描可视化方法便于外科医生和放射科医师与扫描数据进行交互，其包括注释、观察和重新取向，以准确反映成像组织样本在外植前相对于身体的取向。改进的可视化方法包括指示样本取向的颜色编码样本纹理、指示离样本边缘的接近度的颜色编码肿瘤可视化，以及用于调整二维可视化相对于样本的位置和取向的直观方法。
