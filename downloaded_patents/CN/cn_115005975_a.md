## 📝 摘要

The invention discloses a master manipulator clamping device capable of simultaneously superposing force feedback and tactile vibration feedback and a control method thereof, which can intuitively transmit a clamping action command to an instrument at the tail end of a slave manipulator of a surgical robot and accurately feed back the clamping force of the instrument to fingers of an operator in a force feedback manner. An operator can vividly feel the clamping force applied to the tissue by the surgical instrument, so that the immediacy of the operation is enhanced; in addition, vibration stimulation can be applied to fingers of an operator at the same time to carry out overforce reminding, or vibration of different frequencies or amplitudes can be applied according to the hardness and other characteristics of the clamped biological tissue to help the operator to distinguish the tissue type. Or vibration stimulation with different frequencies or amplitudes is applied according to the degree that the surgical instrument deviates from the preset operation track to assist the operator in controlling the slave hand instrument to move along the preset track so as to carry out more accurate surgical operation, and the surgical difficulty is reduced.

**（中文摘要）**

本发明公开了一种能同时叠加力反馈和触觉振动反馈的主手夹持装置及其控制方法，能够直观地将夹取动作命令传递给手术机器人从手末端器械，并将器械夹取力通过力反馈的方式精确反馈到操作人员手指，使操作人员能逼真地感受到手术器械施加到组织的夹取力，增强其操作的临场感；此外，也可以同时通过对操作人员手指施加振动刺激来进行过力提醒，或者根据夹取的生物组织的硬度等特性施加不同频率或幅值的振动来帮助操作人员辨别组织类型，或者根据手术器械偏离预定操作轨迹的程度施加不同频率或幅值的振动刺激来辅助操作人员控制从手器械沿预定轨迹移动以便开展更加精准的手术操作，降低手术难度。
