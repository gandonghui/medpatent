## 📝 摘要

The present disclosure relates to a method of verifying matching of a surgical target, an apparatus for the method, and a system including the apparatus. The method of verifying matching of a surgical target according to the present disclosure comprises the steps of: preparing a 3D model including shape information of a surgical target; acquiring information of the position and the posture of the surgical target and information of the position and the posture of a target mark attached to the surgical target through a tracker; performing a matching with the 3D model by deriving a correlation of position and posture between the surgical target and the target marker; tracking a change in position and posture of a probe tip moving along a surface of the surgical target; and generating match verification information and displaying the match verification information as an image, the match verification information including a 3D graphic icon representing a relative positional relationship between the probe tip and the 3D model, and a change corresponding to at least one of a position and a posture of the probe tip. Therefore, the matching condition is visually and intuitively verified. In addition, the matching degree of a certain continuous area instead of a single point can be intuitively checked.

**（中文摘要）**

本公开涉及一种验证手术目标匹配的方法、用于该方法的装置和包括该装置的系统，根据本公开的验证手术目标匹配的方法包括以下步骤：准备包括手术目标的形状信息的3D模型；通过跟踪器获取所述手术目标的位置和姿势的信息，以及附着在所述手术目标上的目标标记的位置和姿势的信息；通过导出所述手术目标和所述目标标记之间的位置和姿势的相关性，执行与所述3D模型的匹配；跟踪沿所述手术目标的表面移动的探针尖端的位置和姿势的变化；以及生成匹配验证信息，并将匹配验证信息显示为图像，所述匹配验证信息包括3D图形图标，所述3D图形图标表示所述探针尖端和所述3D模型之间的相对位置关系，对应于所述探针尖端的位置和姿势中的至少一个的变化。因此，匹配情况得到了直观和视觉上的验证。此外，可以直观地检查某个连续区域而非单个点的匹配度。
