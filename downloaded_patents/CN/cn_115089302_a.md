## 📝 摘要

The invention relates to a surgical robot system and method, and the system comprises a surgical device which carries a mechanical arm; the navigation system is used for determining a planned motion path of the mechanical arm in the real operation space based on the initial pose of the mechanical arm and the target operation pose, and the planned motion path comprises a motion path and pose change information of the mechanical arm moving along the motion path; and an augmented reality device which is in communication connection with the navigation system and is used for obtaining the planned motion path and displaying the pose change process of the virtual mechanical arm model on the augmented reality device according to the planned motion path, the pose change process of the virtual mechanical arm model comprises a virtual motion path and a virtual pose of the virtual mechanical arm model so as to display the relative position relation with a real operation space object. A user can intuitively know whether the mechanical arm is collided or not and whether the mechanical arm reaches the singular point position or not before the mechanical arm is controlled to move according to the current planned movement path.

**（中文摘要）**

本申请涉及一种手术机器人系统及方法，包括：手术装置，搭载有机械臂；导航系统，用于基于机械臂的起始位姿和目标手术位姿，确定真实手术空间中的机械臂的规划运动路径，规划运动路径包括运动路径和机械臂沿运动路径运动的位姿变化信息；以及增强现实装置，增强现实装置与导航系统通信连接，用于获取规划运动路径，并根据规划运动路径在增强现实装置上显示虚拟机械臂模型的位姿变化过程，虚拟机械臂模型的位姿变化过程包括虚拟机械臂模型的虚拟运动路径和虚拟位姿，以展示与真实手术空间物体的相对位置关系。用户可直观了解以当前规划运动路径控制机械臂运动，是否会发生碰撞以及机械臂到达奇异点位置，控制机械臂运动前可避免该情况。
