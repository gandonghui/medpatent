## 📝 摘要

The invention discloses a construction method of a Nomogram prediction model for mechanical ventilation time prolongation of a patient after a cardiac great vessel operation. The construction method comprises the following steps: S1, data acquisition; s2, statistical analysis; s3, reducing the PMV risk through human intervention; the method comprises the following steps: S1, data acquisition: performing population feature statistics and clinical feature statistics on cardiac great vessel postoperative patients, and dividing the cardiac great vessel postoperative patients into a PMV group A and a non-PMV group B; according to the method, the result of a complex statistical model is singly and intuitively shown, corresponding scores are given to the influence factors of the end point event, the occurrence probability of the end point event is calculated by counting the total score of the factors, medical staff can make decisions and perform perioperative period management on a patient suffering from a heart great vessel operation, and the probability of occurrence of the end point event is calculated. Moreover, reasonable distribution of postoperative medical resources of a cardiac great vascular surgical intensive care inpatient area (CSICU) can be facilitated, and estimation of hospitalization time can be provided for managers, doctors and nurses.

**（中文摘要）**

本发明公开了心脏大血管术后患者机械通气时间延长Nomogram预测模型的构建方法，本构建方法包括以下步骤：S1、数据采集；S2、统计分析；S3、人为干预降低PMV风险；S1、数据采集：对心脏大血管术后患者进行人口特征统计和临床特征统计分为PMV组A和非PMV组B。本发明将复杂的统计模型的结果单一直观地表现出来，赋予终点事件的影响因素相应的分值，通过统计各因素的总分来计算终点事件的发生概率，能够有助于医护人员进行决策以及心脏大血管手术患者的围手术期管理，并且还可以有助于心脏大血管外科重症监护病区(CSICU)术后医疗资源的合理分配，并将为管理人员、医生和护士提供住院时间的估算。
