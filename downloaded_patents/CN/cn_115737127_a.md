## 📝 摘要

The invention relates to the technical field of medical instruments, and discloses a device and method for detecting the positioning precision of the tail end of a surgical robot. Wherein the tail end of the surgical robot is fixedly mounted at one end of the tail end positioner, and the other end of the tail end positioner is fixedly mounted on the detection table; the detection table comprises a base, a detection groove, a plurality of target holes formed in the bottom surface of the detection groove, a plurality of detection probes, a plurality of detection electrode plates and an indicator; one end of the indicator is electrically connected with the base and the other end is electrically connected with the detection probe; after a detection probe penetrates through a positioning hole in the tail end of the surgical robot, the positioning accuracy of the positioning hole in the x-axis direction, the y-axis direction and the z-axis direction is judged according to the contact condition of the bottom end of the detection probe and the target hole in the detection groove and the detection electrode plate. According to the invention, simple probe contact detection is used, and a circuit feedback device is introduced, so that errors can be detected more conveniently and intuitively, the batch detection cost and the detection time are saved, and the detection efficiency is improved; and the detection probe and the detection electrode plate can be replaced.

**（中文摘要）**

本发明涉及医疗器械技术领域，公开了一种手术机器人末端的定位精度的检测装置及检测方法。其中，手术机器人末端固定安装在末端定位器的一端，末端定位器的另一端固定安装至检测台上；检测台包括基座、检测槽、开设于检测槽底面的多个靶孔、多个检测探针、多个检测电极片和指示器；指示器一端电连接基座，另一端电连接检测探针；使用检测探针穿过手术机器人末端的定位孔后，根据检测探针的底端与检测槽内的靶孔、检测电极片的接触情况，判断定位孔在x、y、z轴方向上的定位精度。本发明使用简单的探针接触检测，并引入电路反馈装置，更便捷、直观的对误差进行检测，节省批量检测成本和检测时间，提高检测效率；且检测探针和检测电极片可替换。
