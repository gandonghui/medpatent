## 📝 摘要

According to the bladder cancer patient operation prognosis intelligent pre-judgment system provided by the invention, by evaluating survival prognosis influence factors of bladder cancer patients treated by adopting different operation modes and exploring artificial intelligence model software conversion, known factors can be imported before an operation, and pre-judgment output of survival prognosis of bladder cancer patients of different operation modes can be obtained after model processing; according to the method, the three-year total survival rate corresponding to different patients adopting different surgical treatments can be quantitatively given, the three-year total survival rate corresponding to each patient can be specifically obtained by inputting the numerical value of each influence factor of each patient in software, and the difference of the postoperative long-term survival rates corresponding to different surgical treatments can be calculated, so that the survival rate of each patient can be accurately determined. The method has the characteristics of high accuracy and high quantitative contrast, and has an excellent application prospect in clinical work. According to the system, the patient can intuitively feel the treatment effects of different operations, a doctor and the patient can conveniently optimize a treatment decision together, the pre-operation communication efficiency is improved, and a personalized follow-up visit scheme can be formulated for the patient.

**（中文摘要）**

本发明提供了一种膀胱癌患者手术预后智能预判系统，通过评估采取不同术式治疗膀胱癌患者的生存预后影响因素，探索人工智能模型软件转化，可以实现术前导入已知因素，经模型处理后，得到不同术式膀胱癌患者生存预后的预判输出，可以量化给出不同患者采取不同术式治疗所对应的3年总体生存率，通过在软件内输入每个患者各影响因素的数值，即可针对性得出每个患者所对应3年总体生存率，并可计算出不同术式对应术后远期生存率的差异，具有准确率高及量化对比性强的特点，在临床工作中具有极好的应用前景。本发明的系统使患者能直观感受不同手术的治疗效果，方便医生与患者共同优化治疗决策，提高术前沟通效率，并有助于为患者制定个性化的随访方案。
