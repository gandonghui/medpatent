## 📝 摘要

A control system and control method for controlling an endoscopic surgical robot based on head tracking wherein the control system includes an operation host, a robot and a composite camera wherein the robot clamps an endoscope at a specific position in a specific posture. The composite camera obtains facial features of the physician through a 2D camera on the composite camera so as to identify whether the physician is a registered user, obtains depth information of the physician head through a 3D camera on the composite camera, and confirms whether the 2D information is consistent with the 3D information. The operation host estimates two-dimensional space information and three-dimensional space information corresponding to the head of the physician according to the facial features and the depth information, converts the two-dimensional space information and the three-dimensional space information into robot movement information through a conversion matrix, and then controls the robot to move correspondingly according to the robot movement information. Therefore, the manual dependence on an assistant in an operation can be reduced, and the operation of the endoscope can be more intuitive and convenient.

**（中文摘要）**

一种基于头部追踪控制内视镜手术机器人的控制系统与控制方法，其中该控制系统包括运算主机、机器人以及复合相机，其中机器人以特定姿态夹持内视镜于特定位置。复合相机通过其上的2D相机取得医师的脸部特征，以辨识医师是否为已注册使用者，并通过其上的3D相机取得医师头部的深度信息，并确认2D信息与3D信息是否一致。运算主机借由脸部特征与深度信息估算医师的头部所对应的二维空间信息与三维空间信息，并且借由转换矩阵转换成机器人移动信息后，依据机器人移动信息控制机器人对应移动。据此，可以减少在手术中对于助理的人力依赖，也可令内视镜的操作更为直觉与便利。
