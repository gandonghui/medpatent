## 📝 摘要

The invention discloses a magnetic compatible guide wire for interventional therapy in MRI (Magnetic Resonance Imaging) equipment. The magnetic compatible guide wire comprises a handheld part and a first polytetrafluoroethylene coating, one side of the handheld part is fixedly connected with a head end, a core wire is arranged in the handheld part, and the core wire is made of metal capable of cutting magnetic lines of force; one side of the outer part of the handheld part is coated with a first polytetrafluoroethylene coating, and one end of the outer part of the core wire is provided with a friction reducing structure; the friction reducing structure comprises a second polytetrafluoroethylene coating, one side of the exterior of the core wire is coated with the second polytetrafluoroethylene coating, and a hydrophilic coating is arranged on the other side of the exterior of the core wire; mR fluoroscopy (real-time guided operation) can be carried out, artifacts of the puncture needle have no interference on display of a target area, the relation between the target area and normal tissue can be clearly displayed, and an MR image can display an interventional diagnosis and treatment image almost in real time; therefore, a surgeon can know the position relation between a surgical instrument and a lesion and an important tissue structure at any time, so that the complicated operation is simpler, more convenient and more intuitive.

**（中文摘要）**

本发明公开了一种用于MRI设备中介入治疗用磁兼容导引导丝，包括手持部和第一聚四氟乙烯涂层；手持部的一侧固定连接有头端，手持部的内部设置有芯丝，芯丝由能够切割磁力线的金属制成；手持部的外部一侧涂覆有第一聚四氟乙烯涂层，芯丝外部的一端设置有减少摩擦结构；减少摩擦结构包括第二聚四氟乙烯涂层，第二聚四氟乙烯涂层涂覆在芯丝外部的一侧，芯丝外部的另一侧设置有亲水涂层；可作MR透视(实时导引手术)，穿刺针伪影对靶向区域显示无干扰，能清楚显示靶向区域与正常组织关系，使MR图像达到近乎实时显示介入诊疗影像；使手术医生就能够随时了解手术器械与病变、重要组织结构的位置关系，从而使复杂的操作更加简捷、直观。
