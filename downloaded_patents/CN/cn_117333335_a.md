## 📝 摘要

The invention discloses a urology lithotripsy simulation teaching system and a use method, and relates to the technical field of simulation teaching systems. Comprising a teaching subsystem and an experiment subsystem. Wherein the teaching subsystem comprises a three-dimensional model display module, and the three-dimensional model display module carries out digital processing on a three-dimensional model of a patient, so that a doctor can conveniently carry out simulation training of surgical operation; more intuitive operation reference can be provided for doctors by accurately measuring and analyzing the three-dimensional model of the patient; and the surgical instrument module is used for providing virtualized display of various surgical instruments for doctors. According to the invention, related personnel can be helped to improve surgical skills, and doctors can perform surgical operation training without risks by simulating a real surgical operation environment, so that the surgical skills and clinical experience are improved; surgical operation training is carried out in the virtual environment, medical negligence caused by misoperation can be effectively avoided, and the surgical risk is reduced.

**（中文摘要）**

本发明公开了一种泌尿外科碎石术模拟教学系统及使用方法，涉及模拟教学系统技术领域；包括教学子系统和实验子系统；其中，教学子系统包括：三维模型展示模块，该模块将患者的三维模型进行数字化处理，以便于医生进行手术操作的模拟训练；通过对患者的三维模型进行精确的测量和分析，可以为医生提供更加直观的操作参考；手术器械模块，该模块为医生提供各种手术器械的虚拟化展示。本发明能够帮助相关人员提高手术技能，通过模拟真实的手术操作环境，医生可以在无风险的情况下进行手术操作训练，从而提高手术技能和临床经验；能够降低手术风险：在虚拟环境中进行手术操作训练，可以有效避免因误操作而导致的医疗事故，降低手术风险。
