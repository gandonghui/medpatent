## 📝 摘要

The invention relates to a navigation method and a navigation system for spinal endoscopic surgery. The method comprises the following steps: a three-dimensional image acquisition step: acquiring a three-dimensional image of at least one part of a spine; an image acquisition step: acquiring an image of the endoscope in real time; a direction obtaining step: obtaining the position and the direction of the tail end of the endoscope in real time; an image cutting step: cutting the three-dimensional image according to the position and direction of the tail end of the endoscope; a fusion step: fusing the cut three-dimensional image and the image; and a display step: displaying the fused image. According to the invention, the augmented reality fusion of the real-time endoscope view and the three-dimensional image around the endoscope is realized in the scene of the spinal endoscopic surgery, and the more intuitive azimuth information of the endoscope tail end and the surgical tool tail end in the spinal structure is provided for doctors in the spinal surgery; misoperation is avoided to a great extent, and reliability of spinal surgery is improved.

**（中文摘要）**

本发明涉及一种用于脊柱内窥镜手术的导航方法以及导航系统。所述方法包括如下步骤：三维影像获取步骤：获取脊柱的至少一部分的三维影像；图像获取步骤：实时获取内窥镜的图像；方位获取步骤：实时获取内窥镜末端的位置和方向；影像切割步骤：根据所述内窥镜末端的位置和方向对所述三维影像进行切割；融合步骤：将切割后的三维影像和所述图像进行融合；以及显示步骤：显示融合后的图像。本发明在脊柱内窥镜手术的场景中实现实时内窥镜视野与内窥镜周围的三维影像的增强现实融合，在脊柱手术中为医生提供更直观的内窥镜末端以及手术工具末端在脊柱结构中的方位信息，很大程度上避免误操作并增加脊柱手术的可靠性。
