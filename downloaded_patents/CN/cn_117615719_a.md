## 📝 摘要

The present invention relates to an end tool of a surgical instrument and a surgical instrument equipped with the same, and more particularly, to an end tool of a surgical instrument mounted on a robotic arm or manually operable for use in laparoscopic surgery or various surgeries, and a surgical instrument equipped with the same, the end tool is rotatable in two or more directions and acts intuitively in conformity with the action of the operating portion.

**（中文摘要）**

本发明涉及一种手术器械的末端工具以及配备其的手术器械，具体地，涉及一种为了用于腹腔镜手术或各种手术，安装在机器人手臂上或可手动操作的手术器械的末端工具以及配备其的手术器械，其中，所述手术器械配备末端工具，所述末端工具可在两个或更多个方向旋转并与操作部的动作直观上一致地动作。
