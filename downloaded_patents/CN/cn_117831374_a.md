## 📝 摘要

The invention discloses a liver suturing training simulator under an endoscope, which comprises an opening and closing type shell model, a simulated gall bladder, a three-dimensional mechanical sensor and an electromagnetic tracking device, and is characterized in that the opening and closing type shell model comprises a human abdomen simulating upper shell fixed on a bottom plate; the three-dimensional mechanical sensor and the liver model are fixed in the opening and closing type shell model, the liver model is provided with a simulated gall bladder, a simulated bile duct, a simulated liver internal blood vessel and a simulated liver internal blood vessel branch, a neural network is used for automatically segmenting the liver position, the liver internal blood vessel and the liver internal blood vessel branch from a CT image, and a three-dimensional model is constructed and then is manufactured through 3D printing; the simulated bile duct, the blood vessels in the liver and the branches are filled with simulated liquid; the measurement range of the electromagnetic tracking device covers the working space of the tail end of the suture surgical instrument and is used for tracking the movement track of the tail end of the suture surgical instrument. According to the method, evaluation is more comprehensive, objective and economical, a trainee can be more intuitively guided in details, and the improvement process of operation skills is more targeted.

**（中文摘要）**

本发明公开了一种腔镜下肝脏缝合培训模拟器，包括开合式壳体模型、模拟胆囊、三维力学传感器和电磁追踪装置，开合式壳体模型包括固定在底板上的仿人体腹部上壳；三维力学传感器和肝脏模型固定在开合式壳体模型内，肝脏模型上设置有模拟胆囊、模拟胆管及模拟肝脏内血管及其分支，利用神经网络自动从CT影像中分割肝脏位置和肝脏内血管及其分支，构建三维模型后通过3D打印制成；模拟胆管、肝脏内血管及其分支内灌注仿真液体；电磁追踪装置的测量范围覆盖缝合手术器械末端的工作空间，用于追踪缝合手术器械末端的运动轨迹。利用本发明进行评价更加全面、客观、经济，可以从细节上对受训者进行更为直观的指导，使操作技能的提升过程更具针对性。
