## 📝 摘要

The invention discloses a multi-degree-of-freedom surgical instrument which comprises a tail end executing part, an operating part for controlling the tail end executing part to act, and a transmission part for transmitting the action of the operating part to the tail end executing part, the transmission part comprises at least two guide wires and a plurality of transmission assemblies, each transmission assembly comprises a rotating shaft and a wire guide wheel rotating with the rotating shaft as the center axis, one end of each guide wire is connected with the operation part, and the other end of each guide wire is fixedly arranged relative to the execution assembly. And at least one part of the guide wire middle section is wound on the guide wire wheel, so that the tail end executing part acts in the direction substantially the same as the operation direction of the operation part. The multi-degree-of-freedom surgical instrument can realize intuitive operation and free operation under the condition that the surgical perforation diameter is very small.

**（中文摘要）**

本发明公开了一种多自由度手术器械，包括末端执行部、控制末端执行部动作的操作部，以及将操作部的动作向末端执行部传递的传动部；传动部包括导丝以及若干传动组件，所述传动部包括至少两个导丝以及若干传动组件，各传动组件包括一个旋转轴及以所述旋转轴为中心轴旋转的导线轮，所述导丝一端与所述操作部连接，所述导丝另一端与所述执行组件相对固定设置，所述导丝中间段至少一部分卷绕所述导线轮，以形成所述末端执行部沿与所述操作部的操作方向实质相同的方向进行动作。本发明的多自由度手术器械能在手术穿孔直径很小的情况下实现直观性的运转以及自由操作。
