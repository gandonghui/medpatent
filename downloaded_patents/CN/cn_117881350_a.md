## 📝 摘要

The present invention relates to a tip tool of a surgical instrument and a surgical instrument having the same, and more particularly, to a tip tool of a surgical instrument and a surgical instrument having the same, which can be mounted on a robot arm or can be manually operated for a laparoscopic surgery or various other surgeries, and a tip tool which can be rotated in two or more directions, and which acts intuitively in accordance with the action of the operation unit.

**（中文摘要）**

本发明涉及一种手术用器械的末端工具以及具有其的手术用器械，具体涉及一种手术用器械的末端工具以及具有其的手术用器械，其中，所述手术用器械可以安装在机器人臂上或可以手动操作以用于腹腔镜手术或各种其他手术，并且具有末端工具，所述末端工具可以在两个或多个方向上旋转，并且其动作直观上与操作部的动作一致。
