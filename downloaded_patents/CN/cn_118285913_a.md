## 📝 摘要

The invention relates to a method for guiding an endoscopic surgery under a navigation system, a corresponding computer-readable storage medium, a control device, a computer program product, and an electronic device, a navigation system and a surgical robot system for navigation of an endoscopic surgery. The method comprises the following steps: an endoscope azimuth acquisition step: acquiring the azimuth of an endoscope under a navigation system; a scaleplate model construction step: constructing a scaleplate model according to the orientation of the endoscope under the navigation system; a guide image acquisition step; an enhanced image fusion step: fusing the scaleplate model and the guide image to obtain an enhanced image; and a display step of displaying a view including at least a portion of the enhanced image. As the scaleplate model is fused in the guide image, an operator can more intuitively judge the length of the tool extending out of the working channel and the distance/direction of each physiological structure/point of interest in the image under the endoscope relative to the endoscope and the tool by taking the scaleplate model as a reference, so that the endoscope can be more accurately operated.
