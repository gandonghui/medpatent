## 📝 摘要

The invention discloses a flexible ureteroscope surgical robot with multiple instruments cooperatively operated, and relates to the field of medical surgical robots, and the flexible ureteroscope surgical robot specifically comprises a flexible ureteroscope driving module, a ureter guide sheath driving module, a flexible ureteroscope fast-assembly module, a sheath tube fast-assembly module, a guide wire feeding module, a control box and the like. Firstly, the ureter guiding sheath driving module accurately positions the ureter guiding sheath into the urethra and the ureter of a patient, and conditions are created for unobstructed entering of the disposable flexible lens. Next, the flexible lens driving module drives the disposable flexible lens to enter the body through the ureter guiding sheath which is in place, the guide wire feeding module controls the laser guide wire to be guided in along the flexible lens, and precise laser lithotripsy operation is executed on the position of the calculus. In addition, the lifting module provides necessary height adjustment according to the body condition of the patient, and the adaptability of the operation is improved. The control box is a center of system operation and provides an intuitive operation interface for surgeons.
