## 📝 摘要

The invention discloses a duodenum tube for a surgical robot, a tube indwelling method, a tube indwelling device and a medium, the duodenum tube comprises a body part, a tail end part and a controller, and the tail end part comprises a detection device used for detecting the pH value of an in-vivo space where the tail end part is located; the light emitting device is used for emitting first-state light or second-state light, and the first-state light and the second-state light penetrate from the in-vivo space to the in-vitro space and respectively present a corresponding first visual effect and a corresponding second visual effect so as to indicate the position of the tail end part in the in-vivo space in the in-vitro space; the controller is in communication connection with the detection device and the light-emitting device, and the controller is configured to respond to the numerical range of the pH value and control the light-emitting device to selectively emit the first state light or the second state light. According to the invention, the convenience of catheterization of the duodenum tube in a surgical robot environment is improved, and whether the duodenum tube reaches the duodenum or not is indicated more intuitively.

**（中文摘要）**

本申请公开了一种用于手术机器人的十二指肠管、置管方法、装置和介质，该十二指肠管包括本体部分、末端部分和控制器，其中，末端部分包括：探测装置，用于探测所处体内空间的酸碱度；发光装置，用于发出第一状态光或者第二状态光，第一状态光和第二状态光具备从体内空间穿透至体外空间，并分别呈现对应的第一视觉效果和第二视觉效果，以在体外空间指示末端部分所处体内空间的位置；控制器分别与探测装置和发光装置通信连接，控制器被配置为：响应于酸碱度所处的数值范围，控制发光装置选择性发出第一状态光或者第二状态光。本申请提升了十二指肠管在手术机器人环境中进行置管的便捷性，更直观的指示十二指肠管是否到达十二指肠。
