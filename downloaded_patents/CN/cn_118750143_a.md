## 📝 摘要

The invention discloses a multi-degree-of-freedom in-vivo in-situ biological manufacturing system which comprises a conveying catheter, a printing part and a control part, a conveying channel and a driving guide wire are arranged in the conveying catheter, the action of an operation structure is transmitted through the driving guide wire through a driving structure, and finally the printing part is guided to act. The conveying conduit conveys an in-vivo repair material precursor to the printing part through the in-vivo repair material precursor conveying channel, the printing part prints the in-vivo repair material precursor in an in-vivo to-be-repaired area, and the in-vivo repair material precursor generates a repair material in the in-vivo to-be-repaired area through light irradiation or mixing of multiple in-vivo repair material precursors. And acting on an in-vivo to-be-repaired area. The whole structure of the system is miniaturized, the system can be used for in-vivo in-situ repair in a minimally invasive surgery, the driving guide wire is designed to be combined with the flexible pipe, an in-vivo repair material precursor and a curing light assembly are integrated, and through multi-degree-of-freedom control, intuitive operation and free operation are achieved under the condition that the surgical perforation diameter is very small.

**（中文摘要）**

本发明公开了一种多自由度体内原位生物制造系统，包括输送导管、打印部和控制部，输送导管内置输送通道和驱动导丝，通过驱动结构将操作结构的动作通过驱动导丝传递并最终引导打印部动作，输送导管利用体内修复材料前体输送通道将体内修复材料前体输送至打印部，打印部将体内修复材料前体打印在体内待修复区域，体内修复材料前体在体内待修复区域通过光照射或多种体内修复材料前体混合生成修复材料，并作用于体内待修复区域。本发明系统整体结构小型化，可用于微创手术中进行体内原位修复，系统设计驱动导丝结合柔性管且集成了体内修复材料前体和固化光组件，通过多自由度控制，实现在手术穿孔直径很小情况下直观性的运转以及自由操作。
