## 📝 摘要

The invention provides a registration precision verification method, a surgical robot and a surgical navigation system. The method comprises the following steps: projecting a verification mark to a feature point of a patient by using a structured light module; determining a space coordinate of the verification mark based on structured light, and converting the space coordinate into a model space in combination with a registration relationship to obtain the verification mark in the model space; and judging whether the registration precision meets the requirement or not according to the overlap ratio of the verification mark in the model space and the feature point of the patient. The calibration mark is projected to the patient feature point through the structured light module, the position of the projected calibration mark is collected through the structured light module, and then the calibration mark is converted to the model space in combination with the registration relation and is compared with the position of the corresponding patient feature point in the model space, so that the registration precision is verified conveniently, quickly and intuitively, and the accuracy of registration is improved. And no extra trauma is caused.

**（中文摘要）**

本发明提供一种注册精度验证方法、手术机器人及手术导航系统，该方法包括：利用结构光模块投影校验标记到患者特征点；基于结构光确定所述校验标记的空间坐标，并结合注册关系转换至模型空间中，得到模型空间中的校验标记；根据模型空间中的校验标记与患者特征点的重合度判断注册精度是否满足要求。本发明通过结构光模块投影校验标记到患者特征点，并通过结构光模块采集投影的校验标记的位置，然后结合注册关系转换至模型空间，与模型空间中相应的患者特征点位置进行比对，方便、快捷、直观地验证了注册精度，且不会带来额外创伤。
