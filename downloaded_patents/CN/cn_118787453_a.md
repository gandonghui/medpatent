## 📝 摘要

The invention provides a high-voltage steep pulse tumor ablation visualization system and method based on an augmented reality technology. The system comprises an intraoperative image acquisition device, a calculation processing unit, an AR projection device, a display device and a high-voltage steep pulse generation device. The method comprises the following steps of (S1) intraoperative image acquisition, (S2) data preprocessing, (S3) ablation area segmentation, (S4) three-dimensional reconstruction, (S5) multi-modal image fusion, (S6) AR projection and (S7) tumor ablation surgical operation.The method has the advantages that surgical accuracy is high, intraoperative intuitive perception is enhanced, intraoperative risks are reduced, and surgical safety is improved.

**（中文摘要）**

本发明提供基于增强现实技术的高压陡脉冲肿瘤消融可视化系统及其方法，其系统包括术中影像获取装置、计算处理单元、AR投影装置、显示设备和高压陡脉冲发生装置；其方法包括如下步骤：S1）术中影像获取，S2）数据预处理，S3）消融区域分割，S4）三维重建，S5）多模态影像融合，S6）AR投影，S7）肿瘤消融手术操作。本发明的优点为：高手术精准性，增强术中直观感知，减少术中风险，提升手术安全性。
