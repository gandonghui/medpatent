## 📝 摘要

The invention discloses a deep learning-based image hematoma state recognition method in a minimally invasive puncture surgery and a storage medium. The method comprises the following steps of S100, data acquisition and preprocessing; s200, carrying out model training; and S300, identifying the hematoma in real time. The endoscope image can be processed in real time, the hematoma recognition result can be rapidly output, the real-time requirement in the operation process is met, and therefore the operation efficiency and safety are improved. Through training and optimization of the deep learning model, the hematoma area can be accurately identified and positioned, the state of the hematoma area is judged, misjudgment and missed judgment are reduced, and the diagnosis reliability is improved. According to the method, image processing and hematoma recognition are automatically completed, the workload of doctors is reduced, the working efficiency is improved, and the doctors can concentrate on surgical operations. According to the method, the identification result is displayed on the endoscope image in an intuitive mode, so that a doctor can conveniently refer to and judge in real time, and intuitive operation auxiliary information is provided.
