## 📝 摘要

The invention discloses a visual surgical navigation method and system for neurosurgery, and the method comprises the steps: employing a surgical navigation system to assist in positioning, accurately finding the position of a focus through the calculation of software, and calculating the size of the focus; a doctor selects a proper focus puncture point and a proper approach point, and a computer visually gives path and distance data from the approach point to the focus through multi-mode 3D modeling; and then a computer software system gives out two visual virtual models at different angles through calculation, after the visual virtual models completely coincide with a real patient, a computer gives out two prompt points at different angles, so that a punctured drainage catheter completely coincide with the two prompt points in different directions, and the direction of an approach can be accurately found. Under the condition, the position of the focus, the position of the approach point and the numerical value from the approach point to the focus point are determined, and the computer software system gives the approach angle of the drainage catheter, so that the operation can be accurately, intuitively and quickly performed.

**（中文摘要）**

本发明公开提供了一种神经外科可视化手术导航方法及系统，本发明是采用手术导航系统辅助定位，先通过软件的计算准确找到病灶的位置并计算出病灶的大小。医生通过选择合适的病灶穿刺点和入路点，计算机通过多模态3D建模直观的给出入路点到病灶的路径及距离数据；然后电脑软件系统通过计算给出两个不同角度的可视虚拟模型，通过与真实患者完全重合后，计算机给出两个不同角度的提示点，使其穿刺的引流导管与两个不同方向的提示点完全重合，即可准确的找到了入路的方向。在这种情况下，确定病灶的位置，入路点的位置，入路点到病灶点的数值，电脑软件系统将给出引流导管的入路角度，即可准确直观快速手术。
