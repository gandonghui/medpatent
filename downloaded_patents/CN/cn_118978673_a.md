## 📝 摘要

The invention provides a polycaprolactone material containing a stilbene structure as well as a preparation method and application of the polycaprolactone material, and belongs to the technical field of polycaprolactone. The preparation method of the polycaprolactone material containing the stilbene structure comprises the following steps: reacting a third raw material for 20-36 hours at the temperature of 105-120 DEG C under the protection of inert gas, wherein the weight-average molecular weight of the product is detected to reach 20000-50000 through GPC (Gel Permeation Chromatography). Wherein the third raw material comprises a compound A, epsilon-caprolactone, a catalyst and a second solvent. According to the preparation method, the compound A and epsilon-caprolactone are used as raw materials to prepare the polycaprolactone material containing the stilbene structure, the preparation method is simple and convenient, and the prepared polycaprolactone material containing the stilbene structure is stable in structure, can be used for preparing surgical sutures, tissue engineering scaffolds and drug sustained-release capsules, and has good biocompatibility after being implanted into the corium layer of a human body. And the degradation degree of the material in a human body can be observed very intuitively through an ultraviolet radiation method.

**（中文摘要）**

本申请提供一种含二苯乙烯结构的聚己内酯材料及其制备方法和应用，属于聚己内酯技术领域。含二苯乙烯结构的聚己内酯材料的制备方法包括在惰性气体保护下将第三原料在105℃～120℃的温度下反应20h～36h，通过GPC检测到产物的重均分子量达到20000～50000。其中，第三原料包括化合物A、ε‑己内酯、催化剂和第二溶剂。本申请的制备方法以化合物A和ε‑己内酯为原料制备含二苯乙烯结构的聚己内酯材料，制备方法简便，制得的含二苯乙烯结构的聚己内酯材料结构稳定，且可用于制备外科缝合线、组织工程支架和药物缓释胶囊，其在植入人体真皮层后，可通过紫外照射的方法，非常直观的观察材料在人体内的降解程度。
