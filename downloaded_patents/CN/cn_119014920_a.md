## 📝 摘要

The purpose of the present invention is to provide a surgical machine for intuitively coincide the operation of an actual tip tool or performing a surgery with the operation of a corresponding operation unit. Specifically, for the above purpose, there is provided: a tip tool having a plurality of degrees of freedom; an operation part having a structure capable of intuitively operating the end tool; and a power transmission unit that transmits the driving force of the operation unit to the end tool so that the end tool operates in accordance with the operation of the operation unit.

**（中文摘要）**

本发明的目的在于提供一种手术用机械，其用于使实际末端工具曲折或执行手术的动作与相应的操作部运转直观上一致。具体地，出于上述目的，提供：末端工具，其具有复数个自由度；操作部，其具有能够直观地操作末端工具的结构；动力传递部，其将操作部的驱动力传递至末端工具以使末端工具根据操作部的操作进行动作。
