## 📝 摘要

The invention relates to a control end rotation limiting structure and a method for synchronizing rotation of a control end and a delivery end, and the control end rotation limiting structure comprises a bottom plate; a push rod; the main moving block is arranged on the bottom plate in a reciprocating linear moving manner; the push rod is connected with the main moving block through a transmission assembly. A fixed limiting part and a movable limiting part are arranged on the two sides of the main moving block in the moving direction; and the driving mechanism is connected with the movable limiting part and can drive the movable limiting part to linearly move so as to adjust the distance between the movable limiting part and the fixed limiting part and further limit the number of rotatable turns of the push rod. According to the invention, an operator at the control end can more intuitively feel the state of the delivery end when the interventional surgical instrument is rotated to reach the limit, and corresponding stroke limitation can be carried out according to the diameters of different interventional surgical instruments, so that the push rod for controlling the interventional surgical instrument is in one-to-one correspondence with the rotatable turns of the delivery end all the time.

**（中文摘要）**

本发明为一种控制端旋转限位结构及同步控制端与递送端旋转的方法，其中，控制端旋转限位结构包括：底板；推杆；主移动块，能往复直线移动地设在底板上；推杆通过传动组件与主移动块连接；在主移动块的移动方向两侧设有固定限位部和活动限位部；驱动机构，与活动限位部连接，并能驱动活动限位部直线移动，以调整活动限位部与固定限位部之间的距离，进而限制推杆的可旋转圈数。本发明可以让控制端的操作者更直观地感受递送端在旋转介入手术器械达到限位时的状态，还可以根据不同的介入手术器械的直径进行相应的行程限制，使控制介入手术器械的推杆始终与递送端的可旋转圈数进行一一对应。
