## 📝 摘要

The invention relates to a rotation limiting structure and method for a control end of an interventional operation robot, and the structure comprises a bottom plate; a push rod; the push rod is sleeved with at least two first gears. Two fixed limiting parts are arranged on the two sides of the moving direction of the main moving block; at least two second gears are arranged at one end of the first lead screw; and the driving mechanism can drive all the first gears to linearly move together, so that only one first gear is meshed with the corresponding second gear, and then the number of rotatable turns of the push rod is limited. According to the invention, an operator at the control end can more intuitively feel the state of the delivery end when the rotary interventional surgical instrument is limited, and the corresponding gear transmission ratio adjustment can be carried out according to the diameters of different interventional surgical instruments, so that the push rod for controlling the interventional surgical instrument is in one-to-one correspondence with the rotatable turns of the delivery end all the time.

**（中文摘要）**

本发明为一种介入手术机器人控制端旋转限位结构及方法，其中，介入手术机器人控制端旋转限位结构包括：底板；推杆；在所述推杆上套设有至少两个第一齿轮；主移动块，在所述主移动块的移动方向两侧设有两个固定限位部；在所述第一丝杠的一端设有至少两个第二齿轮；驱动机构，能驱动各所述第一齿轮一起直线移动，以使仅有其中一个所述第一齿轮与对应的所述第二齿轮相啮合，进而限制所述推杆的可旋转圈数。本发明可以让控制端的操作者更直观地感受递送端在旋转介入手术器械达到限位时的状态，还可以根据不同的介入手术器械的直径进行相应的齿轮传动比调整，使控制介入手术器械的推杆始终与递送端的可旋转圈数进行一一对应。
