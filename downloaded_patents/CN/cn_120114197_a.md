## 📝 摘要

The invention relates to the technical field of medical equipment, in particular to a surgical incision edge positioning and marking device which comprises a nail bin clamping plate and a nail abutting seat, a nail bin assembly is arranged on the nail bin clamping plate, and a nail pushing unit is arranged on the nail bin assembly. The nail pushing unit comprises a push rod with one end extending into a handle of the anastomat and an I-shaped tool bit which is arranged on the push rod and is used for cutting off mammary tissue and pushing a suture nail; the marking assembly comprises a marking unit and a feeding unit, wherein the marking unit is arranged on the I-shaped tool bit and used for marking and positioning the surgical cutting edge in the process that the I-shaped tool bit pushes the sewing nail, and the feeding unit is arranged on the I-shaped tool bit and used for conveying dye to the marking unit. And a drying assembly. According to the device, in the stitching nail anastomosis process, dye rolling and coating can be synchronously carried out on the preprocessed surface of a stitching nail, multiple colors can be selected, accurate judgment can be carried out in surgical cutting edge marking and positioning, more intuitive visual help is provided, and overall operation is easy and convenient.

**（中文摘要）**

本发明涉及医疗器械设备技术领域，尤其涉及手术切缘定位标记装置，包括钉仓夹板和抵钉座，所述钉仓夹板上设置有钉仓组件，所述钉仓组件上设置有推钉单元，所述推钉单元包括一端延伸至吻合器手柄内的推杆和设置在所述推杆上用以切下乳腺组织并推进缝钉的工字刀头；标记组件，包括设置在所述工字刀头上并用于在所述工字刀头推进缝钉的过程中对手术切缘进行标记定位的标记单元、设于所述工字刀头上用于向所述标记单元输送染料的供料单元；干燥组件。本发明能够在进行缝钉吻合过程中，同步对缝钉上预处理的表面进行染料滚压涂覆，且可在多种颜色中选择，使得在手术切缘标记定位中进行准确判断，以提供更直观的视觉帮助，整体操作简便。
