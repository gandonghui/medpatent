## 📝 摘要

The invention discloses a multi-modal image surgical navigation method and system, electronic equipment and a medium, and solves the problems that the capability of integrating multi-modal data is insufficient, a clear mechanism for synchronously displaying different modal information is lacked, and the comprehensive understanding and accurate operation of a surgeon on a tissue structure in a surgery are limited. By integrating a deep learning organ segmentation model, an esophageal model registration algorithm and a manual adjustment mechanism, the limitation of the prior art in esophageal surgery application is solved. The system can effectively process dynamic changes of the esophagus in the operation process, continuous and accurate navigation information is provided, an intuitive and interactive operation environment is created for surgeons, and the accuracy and safety of the operation are further improved.

**（中文摘要）**

本发明公开了一种多模态影像手术导航方法、系统、电子设备及介质，解决存在的整合多模态数据方面能力不足，缺乏清晰机制来同步展示不同模态信息，限制了外科医生对手术中组织结构的全面理解与精准操作的问题。本发明通过集成深度学习器官分割模型、食管模型配准算法、以及手动调整机制，解决了现有技术在食管手术应用中的局限性。系统能够有效处理食管在手术过程中的动态变化，提供持续精确的导航信息，为外科医生营造直观、交互式的手术环境，进一步提升手术的精准度与安全性。
