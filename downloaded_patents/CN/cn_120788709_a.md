## 📝 摘要

The invention relates to a bipolar plasma excitation intelligent switching system which comprises a bipolar electrode unit, the output end of the bipolar electrode unit is electrically connected with an impedance detection module, the output end of the impedance detection module is electrically connected with a microcontroller, and the output end of the microcontroller is electrically connected with an execution unit. The output end of the execution unit is electrically connected with a temperature compensation module. By means of intelligent impedance matching, dynamic temperature compensation and a multi-stage safety protection mechanism, operation accuracy and safety are remarkably improved, energy output can be automatically optimized according to different tissue characteristics and operation types through the self-adaptive learning ability of the system, the system is matched with an intuitive man-machine interaction interface, operation difficulty is lowered, and operation efficiency is improved. And meanwhile, the layered hardware architecture and the electromagnetic shielding design ensure the stable operation of the system in a complex operation environment, and a more intelligent and more reliable energy management scheme is provided for the surgical operation.

**（中文摘要）**

本申请涉及一种双极等离子激发智能切换系统，其包括双极电极单元，所述双极电极单元的输出端电性连接有阻抗检测模块，所述阻抗检测模块的输出端电性连接有微控制器，所述微控制器的输出端电性连接有执行单元，所述执行单元的输出端电性连接有温度补偿模块。本申请通过智能阻抗匹配、动态温度补偿和多级安全保护机制，显著提升了手术精确度和安全性，其自适应学习能力可根据不同组织特性和手术类型自动优化能量输出，配合直观的人机交互界面，不仅降低了操作难度，还减少了组织碳化和电极粘连风险，同时分层式硬件架构和电磁屏蔽设计确保了系统在复杂手术环境中的稳定运行，为外科手术提供了更智能、更可靠的能量管理方案。
