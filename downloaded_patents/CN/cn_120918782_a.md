## 📝 摘要

The invention discloses an endoscopic surgery system, which relates to the field of surgical equipment and comprises a multi-axis surgical robot and a surgical instrument, the multi-axis surgical robot comprises a base and a joint assembly, the base is installed in the area where the operating table is located, the head end of the joint assembly is connected with the base, and the tail end is connected with a surgical instrument; the surgical instrument comprises a main shell, a first fingerstall, a second fingerstall, a transmission wheel mechanism and a clamping jaw. The first fingerstall and the second fingerstall are rotationally connected to one end of the main shell and are in transmission connection with the input end of the transmission wheel mechanism through a traction assembly. The clamping jaw is connected to the other end of the main shell in an opening and closing mode and is in transmission connection with the output end of the transmission wheel mechanism. The joint assembly is driven by the main shell to passively move; when the first fingerstall and the second fingerstall get away from each other or get close to each other, the traction assembly drives the transmission wheel mechanism to rotate according to different rotation modes, so that the clamping jaw is opened or closed. According to the scheme, the problem that an existing operation system is difficult to achieve rapid and intuitive instrument adjustment and accurate clamping can be solved.

**（中文摘要）**

本发明公开了一种腔镜手术系统，涉及手术设备领域，包括多轴手术机器人和手术器械；多轴手术机器人包括基座和关节组件，基座安装于手术台所在区域，关节组件的首端连接基座、末端连接手术器械；手术器械包括主壳体、第一指套、第二指套、传动轮机构和夹爪，第一指套和第二指套转动连接于主壳体一端并通过牵引组件与传动轮机构的输入端传动连接；夹爪可开合地连接于主壳体另一端并与传动轮机构的输出端传动连接；关节组件在主壳体的带动下被动运动；第一指套与第二指套相互远离或靠近时，通过牵引组件带动传动轮机构按不同的转动模式转动，以实现夹爪的张开或闭合。本方案可解决现有的手术系统难以实现快速、直觉式的器械调整与精准夹持的问题。
