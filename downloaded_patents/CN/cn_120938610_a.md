## 📝 摘要

The invention relates to a surgical robot, a main operation table, a posture adjusting mechanism, an adjusting part of the posture adjusting mechanism and a spherical pair structure. The posture adjusting mechanism comprises a first adjusting part; a second adjusting part; and a coupling portion; the postures of the first adjusting part and the second adjusting part are adjustable, the first adjusting part and the second adjusting part are detachably coupled through detachable connection with the coupling part, when the first adjusting part and the second adjusting part are disconnected, the postures of the first adjusting part and the second adjusting part can be adjusted respectively, or when the first adjusting part and the second adjusting part are coupled, the postures of the first adjusting part and the second adjusting part can be adjusted respectively. And the first adjusting part and the second adjusting part can be adjusted in a linkage manner to ensure that the first adjusting part and the second adjusting part are always basically parallel through basically consistent posture changes. Through coupling of the first adjusting part and the second adjusting part in the posture adjusting mechanism, the imaging part and the reference part can be always basically parallel, and intuitive control of the input part based on the imaging part can be achieved.

本发明涉及一种手术机器人、主操作台、姿态调节机构及其调节部、球副结构。姿态调节机构包括：第一调节部；第二调节部；及耦合部；第一调节部和第二调节部的姿态均可调，第一调节部和第二调节部通过与耦合部可分离的连接以可断开的耦接，在第一调节部和第二调节部断开耦接时，第一调节部和第二调节部可分别调节姿态，或者，在第一调节部和第二调节部耦接时，第一调节部和第二调节部可联动调节以通过基本一致的姿态变化确保两者始终基本保持平行。利用该姿态调节机构中第一调节部和第二调节部的耦接，可以使成像部和基准部始终基本平行，能够实现输入部基于成像部进行直觉控制。
