## 📝 摘要

The invention provides a gesture control system and an interaction method for a sterile operation. The gesture control system comprises a visualization module and an interaction module, the visualization module is composed of a personal computer provided with visualization software and a display screen and used for loading and displaying the three-dimensional image model of the patient; and the hardware of the interaction module is an Ultra < 3 > Di three-dimensional hand tracking camera, and the interaction module is connected to the personal computer and is used for capturing hand actions. The system is not only suitable for surgical operations, but also can be used for classroom education of medical students, and helps the medical students to more intuitively understand the anatomical structures of organs. Meanwhile, due to the modular architecture and expandability of the system, a wide space is provided for future scientific research and development and technology upgrading, and the refined and intelligent progress of surgical operations can be promoted.

**（中文摘要）**

本发明提供一种面向无菌手术的手势控制系统及交互方法，包括可视化模块和交互模块；可视化模块，由装有可视化软件的个人计算机和显示屏组成，用于加载并展示患者的三维影像模型；交互模块，硬件为Ultraleap 3Di立体手部追踪相机，连接至所述个人计算机，用于捕捉手部动作。本发明系统不仅适用于外科手术，还可用于医学生的课堂教育，帮助他们更直观地理解脏器解剖结构。同时，系统的模块化架构和可扩展性，为未来的科研发展和技术升级提供了广阔的空间，有助于推动外科手术的精细化和智能化进程。
