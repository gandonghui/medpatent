## 📝 摘要

The invention discloses a surgical instrument cleaning quality detection device, and belongs to the field of medical instruments, the surgical instrument cleaning quality detection device comprises a lower cylinder, an upper cylinder and a middle cylinder, the middle cylinder is arranged above the lower cylinder, the upper cylinder is arranged above the middle cylinder, a placing disc is rotatably arranged in the lower cylinder, a filter plate is arranged in the middle cylinder and at the bottom of the lower cylinder, the lower cylinder comprises a first outer cylinder and a first inner cylinder, and the first outer cylinder and the first inner cylinder are connected through a connecting rod. The first inner cylinder is arranged on the inner side of the first outer cylinder, a first through opening is formed in the bottom of one side wall of the inner cylinder, a first clamping groove is formed in the top of one side wall of the outer cylinder, a sliding groove is formed in the inner wall of the first inner cylinder, the sliding groove is annularly formed, a connecting pipe is arranged on one side wall of the outer cylinder and communicates with the first inner cylinder, and a shifting rod is arranged on the inner wall of the first inner cylinder. The plurality of shifting rods extend downwards in an L shape, and the shifting rods are arranged above the sliding groove. According to the invention, the cleaning quality of the surgical instrument can be more intuitively detected.

本发明公开了一种手术器械清洗质量检测装置，属于医疗器械领域，包括下筒、上筒和中筒，所述中筒设于下筒上方，所述上筒设于中筒上方，所述下筒内转动设有放置盘，所述中筒内与下筒底部设有过滤板，所述下筒包括外筒一与内筒一，所述内筒一设于外筒一内侧，所述内筒一侧壁底部开设有通口一，所述外筒一侧壁顶部设有卡槽一，所述内筒一内壁设有滑槽，所述滑槽环形设置，所述外筒一侧壁设有连接管，所述连接管连通内筒一，所述内筒一内壁设有拨杆，所述拨杆设有多个且呈L型向下延伸，所述拨杆设于滑槽上方。本发明能够更加直观地检测出手术器械清洗质量。
