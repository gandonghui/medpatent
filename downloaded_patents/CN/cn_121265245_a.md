## 📝 摘要

The invention provides a method for planning coronary heart disease intervention based on hemodynamics, relates to the technical field of coronary heart disease surgical intervention, and aims to provide a coronary heart disease intervention planning method through coronary artery hemodynamics analysis based on computational fluid mechanics on the basis of reconstructing a coronary artery accurate three-dimensional model of a patient. Morphological adjustment is carried out on various surgical intervention modes on a patient coronary artery three-dimensional model before an operation, then numerical solution is carried out through spatial discretization and grid division by taking a Navier Stokes equation as a control equation so as to obtain a haemodynamic analysis result, and then, through key haemodynamic parameters including the local blood flow velocity, the coronary artery three-dimensional model of a patient is calculated. The method can evaluate hemodynamic changes of lesion positions caused by different operation schemes according to the parameters, wall shear stress and fractional flow reserve, and further provides a scientific, effective, intuitive and non-invasive auxiliary diagnosis and treatment mode for clinical treatment of coronary heart disease.

**（中文摘要）**

本发明提供了一种基于血流动力学规划冠心病介入的方法，涉及冠心病外科介入技术领域，通过基于计算流体力学的冠脉血流动力学分析，在重建病人冠脉精准三维模型的基础上，术前对各种外科介入方式在病人冠脉三维模型上进行形态学调整，而后通过空间的离散化与网格划分，以纳维斯托克斯方程作为控制方程进行数值求解，以获得血流动力学分析结果，进而通过关键血流动力学参数，包括局部血流速度，壁面切应力，以及血流储备分数来评估不同的手术方案对于病变位置造成的血流动力学改变，进而为临床治疗冠心病提供一种科学有效且直观无创的辅助诊疗方式。
