## 📝 摘要

The utility model discloses a surgical stitcher device of round tube type with the function of synchronous supervision, including a plurality of pin anvil components and a stitcher body. The pin anvil components include a pin anvil, a pin anvil shaft, and a pin anvil cover. The stitcher body includes a fixed handle, a trigger handle, a pin anvil drawbar, a pin pushing bar and a silk pole. The model is characterized in that the top of the pin anvil cover is equipped with an infrared emission tube, the inside of the stitcher is provided with a driving device which connects the infrared emission tube via conductors to drive the infrared emission tube. The stitcher device illuminates the surgical region by emitting infrared radiation, so that doctors can get every detail in the operation through the display of the auxiliary infrared detection device, intuitively identify the distance that needs stitching and accurately judge the stitching effects.
