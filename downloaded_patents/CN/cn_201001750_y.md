## 📝 摘要

The utility model discloses a surgical stitcher device of round tube type, which includes pin anvil components and a stitcher body. The pin anvil components and the stitcher body are in removable connections through a pin anvil drawbar and a pin anvil shaft. The stitcher is characterized in that the inner wall of the pin anvil is equipped with a circuit board, the circuit board is installed with illuminating components, and a power supply is located inside or outside of the stitcher, and the cover of the pin anvil shaft is covered with reflective materials. The stitcher device is of the illuminating function. Operators use the illuminating components to lighten the operational region, so that they can intuitively get every detail in the operation, accurately judge the anastomosing effect, guarantee the operation accuracy and improve the efficiency.
