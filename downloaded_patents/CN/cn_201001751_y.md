## 📝 摘要

The utility model discloses a surgical stitcher device of round tube type, which includes pin anvil components and a stitcher body. The pin anvil components and the stitcher body are in removable connections through a pin anvil drawbar and a pin anvil shaft. The stitcher is characterized in that the inside of the pin anvil drawbar is installed with an infrared emission tube, the pin anvil shaft is provided with a translucent structure, and the surrounding of the infrared emission tube is provided with a translucent hole or infrared translucent material, and the outside of the LED is provided with translucent holes or translucent materials. A power supply is installed in the pin anvil drawbar, which includes a sealed battery compartment and batteries. The batteries are put in the battery compartment, the bottoms of the batteries are covered with insulating films. One end of the insulating films is exposed from the slotted pin anvil drawbars, and the conductors from the battery compartment connect the infrared emission tube forming a circuit The stitcher device illuminates the surgical region by emitting strong infrared radiation, so that doctors can get every detail in the operation through the display of the auxiliary infrared detection device and intuitively identify the distance that needs stitching, accurately judge the stitching effects, and guarantee the operation accuracy and improve the efficiency.
