## 📝 摘要

The utility model discloses a surgical stitcher device of round tube type, which includes pin anvil components and a stitcher body. The pin anvil components and the stitcher body are in removable connections through a pin anvil drawbar and a pin anvil shaft. The stitcher is characterized in that the inner wall of the pin anvil is equipped with a circuit board, the circuit board is installed with an infrared emission tube, and a power supply is located inside or outside of the stitcher, and the cover of the pin anvil shaft is covered with reflective materials. The stitcher device possesses the function of emitting infrared rays and can sense infrared rays relected from mechanisms, pins and apparatuses, etc. through an infrared ray detection apparatus, so that operators can intuitively get every detail in the operation through the display of the auxiliary infrared detection device, accurately judge the stitching effects, and guarantee the operation accuracy and improve the efficiency.
