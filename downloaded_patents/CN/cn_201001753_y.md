## 📝 摘要

The utility model discloses a surgical stitcher device of round tube type, which includes pin anvil components and a stitcher body. The stitcher body consists of a stitcher pinhead component and a handle. The pin anvil components and the stitcher body are in removable connections through a pin anvil drawbar and a pin anvil shaft. The stitcher is characterized in that the inside of the stitcher knife cavum is installed with an infrared emission tube, a power supply is located inside the stitcher, and the infrared emission tube connects the power supply through conductors forming a circuit. When the stitcher device is used for surgeries, the infrared radiation tube illuminates the surgical region by emitting strong infrared radiation and enables observations by auxiliary infrared detection device, so that doctors can intuitively identify the distance that needs stitching, accurately judge the stitching effects, and guarantee the operation accuracy.
