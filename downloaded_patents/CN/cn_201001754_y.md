## 📝 摘要

The utility model relates to a surgery stitcher of round tube type, belonging to the medical instrument field. The stitcher includes pin anvil components and a stitcher body. The pin anvil components include a pin anvil, a pin anvil shaft, and a pin anvil cover. The pin anvil shaft and the pin anvil are in fixed connection, and the pin anvil cover is installed on the pin anvil. The stitcher body consists of a pin cylinder, a pin anvil drawbar, a pin pushing bar and a handle. The pin anvil components and the stitcher body are in removable connections through a pin anvil drawbar and a pin anvil shaft. The stitcher is characterized in that the pin anvil shaft is provided with light holes, the inside of the pin anvil shaft is installed with an infrared emission tube, the stitcher is provided internally or externally with a power supply, and the infrared emission tube connects the power supply through conductors forming a circuit. The stitcher device can emit infrared rays, so that doctors can through the display screen of the infrared detection device intuitively identify the distance that needs stitching during surgeries, judge the stitching effects, guarantee the operation accuracy, nd avoid surgical errros due to the different experiences and levels of operators.
