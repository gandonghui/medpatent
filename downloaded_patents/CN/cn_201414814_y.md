## 📝 摘要

The utility model relates to an operation power device capable of cooling a knife tool in time. The operation power device comprises a main controller, a foot controller and an application part; the main controller comprises a power supply circuit, a monolithic processor, a master control circuit, a high-power motor driving circuit and a high-speed micro-motor driving circuit; the application partrespectively comprises a high-power motor, high-speed micro-motor, as well as surgical manipulaters and surgical knives which are respectively connected with the high-power motor and the high-speed micro-motor in a matched manner; and the operation power device is characterized in that the main controller further comprises a cooling motor driving circuit and a touch screen, and the application part further comprises a cooling motor connected with the cooling motor driving circuit. The utility model has the advantages that the operation power device is particularly adapted to the circumstancerequiring the two appliances used alternately; the operation time can be effectively reduced by additionally arranging the cooling system, and the operation risk is lowed; and the touch type color liquid crystal display technology is adopted, so that the function switching and the rotational speed display are accurate and intuitive, and the operation is simple.
