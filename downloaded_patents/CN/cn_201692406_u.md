## 📝 摘要

The utility model discloses an anti-infection three-way bar, which comprises a plurality of three-way bar units in parallel array. A transparent external box is sleeved outside the three-way bar units, and the three-way bar units are integrally formed. The anti-infection three-way bar can substantially realize sterile operation, is high in anti-pollution capacity, easy for fixation and difficult to fall off, and allows a patient to move freely. The three-way bar becomes a fully closed integration by the aid of mold opening technique, is difficult to fall off or leak during infusion, guarantees safety of medicine usage by adding secondary protection of the protective box, is integrally transparent and intuitive and extremely simple and convenient in operation, greatly saves surgical time and improves controllability of the infusion process.
