## 📝 摘要

The utility model discloses a breast duct endoscope lens, which comprises a needle-like lens body, wherein scales are arranged on a needle rod of the lens body along the axial direction. The scales are set as readable numbers. The 0 scale of the readable numbers is set at the tip of the needle-like lens body. During examination, an endoscope enters from a nipple to show a tumor, and the length of entering the nipple can be directly read from the needle rod of the lens body, so that the surgical range is intuitively determined; and the lens is fast and convenient to use and high in accuracy.
