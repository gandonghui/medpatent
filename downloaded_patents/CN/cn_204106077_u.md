## 📝 摘要

The utility model discloses a visual testicle descending tunnel expander with a suction device The visual testicle descending tunnel expander with the suction device is characterized in that the visual testicle descending tunnel expander with the suction device comprises a tunnel expander, a retractable suction device and a laparoscope, wherein the retractable suction device and the laparoscope are arranged in the tunnel expander. The visual testicle descending tunnel expander with the suction device not only can guarantee a clear surgical field of view and intuitive surgery, but also can find abnormal situations such as bleeding, so that the surgery is enabled to be safe and reliable and the success rate of the surgery is improved.
