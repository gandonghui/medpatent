## 📝 摘要

The utility model provides an anti-confusion medicine dispensing device special for surgical nursing. The anti-confusion medicine dispensing device comprises a supporting plate, a card containing groove and an indicator lamp. A card placing groove is fixedly formed in one side of the top of the supporting plate; and an indicating lamp is fixedly mounted at the top of the supporting plate. An indicating lamp and a card placing groove are arranged; convenient use, during use, different medicines are respectively placed in the medicine placing grooves; writing names and dosages of medicines on the corresponding paper strips and putting the paper strips into the card placing grooves; the medicine can be conveniently searched in the later period; more convenient and intuitive, when the medicineneeds to be taken out for dispensing; taking out the medicine according to the name and dosage of the medicine; after each kind of medicine is taken out, the indicating lamp switch on one side of thecorresponding supporting plate is pressed down, and the indicating lamp is turned on at the moment, so that the medicine can be taken out, even if other work needs emergency treatment in the medicinedispensing process, nursing personnel cannot influence continuous medicine dispensing after treatment, and the medicine dispensing device is more convenient to use.

**（中文摘要）**

本实用新型提供一种外科护理专用的防混淆配药装置，包括托板、卡片放置槽和指示灯；所述托板的顶部一侧固定设置有一个卡片放置槽；所述托板的顶部固定安装有一个指示灯。指示灯和卡片放置槽的设置，有利于使用的方便性，使用时将不同的药物分别放置在药物放置槽中，然后将对应的纸条写上药物的名称和用量并放入卡片放置槽中，方便后期对药物进行查找，更加方便直观，当需要取出药物进行发放时，根据药物名称和用量将药物取出，在取出每一种药物后将对应的托板一侧的指示灯开关按下，这时指示灯会点亮，这样即可代表此种药物已经取出，即使在配药过程中有其他工作需要紧急处理时，护理人员在处理完后也不会影响继续配药，使用更加方便。
