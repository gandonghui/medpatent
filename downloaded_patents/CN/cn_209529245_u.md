## 📝 摘要

A vagina gauze filling device used after puncture ovum extraction comprises a hemostasis part and an observation part which is exposed out of a vaginal orifice to observe the hemostasis condition in use, and one end of the observation part is fixedly connected with one end of the hemostasis part; the hemostasis part comprises two first gauze bodies which are stacked up and down, and angelica sinensis layers are attached to the first gauze bodies; the peripheries of the two first gauze bodies are tightly connected through a fixing device so that a cavity used for containing hemostatic can be formed between the two first gauze bodies. The observation part comprises a long-strip-shaped second gauze body, one end of the second gauze body is fixedly connected with one end of the hemostasis part, and the other end of the second gauze body is a free end. Compared with the conventional medical gauze, the free end of the second gauze body is exposed out of the vaginal orifice, so that the use frequency of the vaginal speculum and the cervix uteri forceps is reduced, the pain of a patient caused by surgical instruments is reduced, and the condition of a deep puncture point of the vagina canbe intuitively known by observing the bleeding degree of the exposed second gauze body.

**（中文摘要）**

一种穿刺取卵术后用阴道填塞纱布装置，装置包括止血部和使用时外露于阴道口以观察止血状况的观察部，且观察部的一端与止血部的一端固定相连；止血部包括上下叠设的两个第一纱布体，第一纱布体上附着有当归层；两个第一纱布体的周边通过固定装置紧密相连，以使两个第一纱布体之间形成用于放置止血药的空腔；观察部包括呈长条状的第二纱布体，第二纱布体的一端与止血部的一端固定相连，第二纱布体的另一端是自由端。本实用新型与常规医用纱布相比，由于第二纱布体的自由端外露于阴道口，减少了窥阴器及宫颈钳的使用频率，即减少了手术器械给患者带来的疼痛，还可通过观察外露的第二纱布体的渗血程度，直观的了解阴道深处穿刺点的状况。
