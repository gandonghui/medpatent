## 📝 摘要

The utility model provides an instrument mounting rack. Connecting rod, an instrument mounting hole and a lens mounting hole are formed in one end of the connecting rod in the axial direction. A lensis fixedly arranged in the lens installation hole, an instrument is inserted in the instrument installation hole, a groove is formed in the other end of the connecting rod, a locking device is installed in the groove and locks the instrument, the end, close to the instrument, of the locking device is connected to the connecting rod in a pivoted mode, and the other end of the locking device is clamped in the groove. Simple structure, manufacturing and mounting convenience, surgical use, the lens with the structure transmits the end part of an instrument for operation to the screen for image presentation, so that medical personnel can conveniently observe the working condition of the working end of the instrument, the focus observation area is more intuitive, and meanwhile, the focus observation of a doctor can be clearer by virtue of an image amplification technology, and the lens is convenient to popularize and wide in implementation prospect.

**（中文摘要）**

本实用新型提供的一种器械安装架，包括连接杆，所述连接杆一端内部沿轴向开设有器械安装孔和镜头安装孔，所述镜头安装孔中固设有镜头，所述器械安装孔中插设有器械，所述连接杆另一端开设有凹槽，所述凹槽中安装有锁紧装置，所述锁紧装置锁紧所述器械，所述锁紧装置靠近所述器械的一端枢轴连接于所述连接杆，其另一端卡设于所述凹槽。结构简单，制造安装方便，在外科手术使用时，通过本结构的镜头将进行作业的器械端部，传达至屏幕上进行影像呈现，便于医护人员观察器械工作端的工作时的状况，使得观察病灶区更为直观，同时，影像的放大技术同样可以使得医生观察病灶更为清晰，便于推广，实施前景广阔。
