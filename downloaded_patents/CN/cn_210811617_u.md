## 📝 摘要

The utility model provides a microendoscope device for a neurosurgery operation. The operating microscope comprises an operating microscope body, a neuroendoscope and a mounting mechanism, wherein theoperation microscope body comprises a support and a microscope installed on the support, a cylinder is arranged on the lower portion of the microscope, an objective lens is arranged at the lower endof the cylinder, a cylindrical microscope handle is arranged on the upper portion of the neuroendoscope, a video interface is arranged at the upper end of the microscope handle and directly faces andis connected with the objective lens, and the installation mechanism is used for fixedly installing the neuroendoscope on the objective lens. Due to the adoption of the technical scheme, the possibility that a patient is injured by the neuroendoscope is reduced; a surgeon can observe a surgical site from an ocular lens of the microscope without observing through a display screen, and can directlyobserve a more intuitive three-dimensional image from the microscope, so that the surgical accuracy is improved; when an intracranial operation is carried out, a place which cannot be observed by onlyusing the operation microscope body can be observed, so that the operation effect is improved.

**（中文摘要）**

本实用新型提供一种神经外科手术显微内镜装置，包括手术显微镜本体、神经内镜和安装机构，手术显微镜本体包括支架和安装在支架上的显微镜，显微镜的下部设有圆筒，圆筒的下端设有物镜，神经内镜的上部设有圆筒形的镜柄，镜柄的上端设有视频接口，视频接口与物镜正对相接，安装机构用于将神经内镜固定安装在物镜上。由于采用了上述技术方案，本实用新型降低了神经内镜伤害到患者的可能性；手术医生能够从显微镜的目镜观察到手术部位，不用通过显示屏观察，直接从显微镜内观察到更加直观的三维图像，提高了手术的精准度；在进行颅内手术时，能够观察到仅仅使用手术显微镜本体无法观察到的地方，提高了手术效果。
