## 📝 摘要

The utility model discloses a rehabilitation training device for surgical nursing in the pneumology department, relates to the field of rehabilitation training devices, and aims to solve the problems that most existing respiratory rehabilitation training devices do not have a digital display function and are not provided with a filtering device, so that a patient cannot visually see data changes, and the operation is inconvenient. In order to solve the problems that in the prior art, an air inlet is formed in the front surface of the installation base, an air outlet is formed in the side face of the installation base, and a first air column is installed on the upper surface of the installation base, the air inlet is formed in the front surface of the installation base, and the air outlet is formed in the side face of the installation base. The air purifier is novel in structure, the air filter screens are arranged on the air inlet and the air outlet respectively, large particles in air can be filtered out, irritation to a patient is reduced, meanwhile, the flow detector is arranged, each inspiration flow can be detected, data are displayed on the display screen through the microprocessor in the operation panel, comparison is carried out, and the air purifier is convenient to use. And the patient can see the rehabilitation effect more intuitively.

**（中文摘要）**

本实用新型公开了一种用于呼吸科手术护理的康复训练装置，涉及康复训练装置领域，针对现有的呼吸康复训练装置大都不具备数显功能，且不配备过滤装置，导致病人不能直观的看到数据变化，且空气中的大颗粒可能导致病人病情加重的问题，现提出如下方案，其包括安装基座，所述安装基座的正表面安装有进气口，所述安装基座的侧面安装有出气口，所述安装基座的上表面安装有第一气柱。本实用新型结构新颖，且在进气口和出气口均配备有空气过滤网，可以过滤掉空气中的大颗粒，减少对病人的刺激性，同时配备有流量检测器，可以对每次吸气流量进行检测，并通过操作面板内部的微处理器将数据显示在显示屏上，进行比对，使病人更加直观的看到康复效果。
