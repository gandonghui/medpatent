## 📝 摘要

The utility model provides an auxiliary device for inserting a surgical probe. The auxiliary device comprises a flexible material plate, a base and an angle measuring column. The flexible material plate is made of a biocompatible material; the base comprises a cardan shaft, the cardan shaft is fixed in the base, and the ground of the angle measuring column is fixed with the cardan shaft; the base is embedded in the center of the flexible material plate, the flexible material plate comprises at least one through hole, and the through holes are formed in the periphery of the base; the side face of the base comprises an angle indicator. According to the utility model, the flexible material plate and the angle measuring column are matched with the universal shaft, so that multi-angle conversion can be carried out on a plane to adapt to various needle inserting environments; scale marks and color marks are arranged on the measuring column, and an accurate angle reminder is arranged on the base, so that a user can intuitively and conveniently confirm the needle inserting depth and the needle inserting angle.

**（中文摘要）**

本实用新型提供一种手术探针入针辅助设备，包括柔性材料板、基座、量角柱；柔性材料板由生物相容性材料制成；基座包括万向轴，万向轴固定在基座的内部，量角柱的地面与万向轴固定；基座嵌接在柔性材料板的中心，柔性材料板包括至少1个通孔，通孔开设在基座的四周；基座的侧面包括角度指示器；本实用新型通过柔性材料版和量角柱配合万向轴可在平面上进行多角度变换，适应多种入针环境；量柱上设有刻度标识和颜色标识，基座上设有精准的角度提醒，对使用者来说可以直观方便的确认入针深度和入针角度。
