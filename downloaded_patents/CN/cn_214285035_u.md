## 📝 摘要

The length-adjustable suture line comprises a left line and a right line, V-shaped thorns opposite in direction are arranged on the left line and the right line, arc-shaped needles are arranged at one ends of the left line and the right line, the ends, away from the arc-shaped needles, of the left line and the right line are connected through an adjusting mechanism, and the adjusting mechanism comprises a hollow cylinder with two open ends. A supporting block is arranged in the barrel, a clamping block is arranged at the upper end of the supporting block, an adjusting belt is connected to the outer wall of the barrel, and a clamping groove is formed in the adjusting belt; when the adjusting belt is inserted into the cylinder body, the clamping block is clamped in the clamping groove, and the adjusting belt cannot retreat; the free end of the adjusting belt is connected with the right line end part; and the cylinder is connected with a left line. According to the medical length-adjustable double-color double-head absorbable fishbone line provided by the utility model, the colors are different, so that a left branch and a right branch can be intuitively distinguished; the length ratio can be adjusted to be 1: 1, 1: 3 or even 1: 4, different anastomosis modes of operators with different habits can be matched, and surgical operation is facilitated.

**（中文摘要）**

本实用新型公开了一种可调节长度的缝合线，包括左线和右线，左线和右线上设有方向相反的V形刺，左线和右线的其中一端均设有弧形针，左右线远离弧形针的一端通过调节机构连接，调节机构包括中空两端开口的筒体，筒体内部设有支撑块，支撑块上端设有卡块，筒体外壁上连接有调节带，调节带上设有卡槽；调节带插入至筒体内时，卡块卡接于卡槽内，调节带不能退出；调节带的自由端连接连接右线端部；筒体上连接左线。本实用新型提供了一种医用可调节长度的双色双头可吸收鱼骨线，颜色不同有利于直观的区分左右支；长度比例可调节为1:1、1:3甚至1:4，可匹配不同习惯术者的不同吻合方式，利于手术操作。
