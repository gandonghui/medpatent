## 📝 摘要

The utility model discloses a blood-water separation negative pressure suction device. Comprising a liquid guide tube, a liquid discharge tube, an effusion collector, a negative pressure suction tube and a negative pressure suction device, the effusion collector is provided with a plasma collecting cavity and a water collecting cavity; a liquid guide opening connected to the water collecting cavity is formed in the bottom of the plasma collecting cavity; a plasma separation membrane used for blocking plasma and enabling water to pass through is mounted at the liquid guide opening; the liquid discharge pipe is connected between the liquid guide pipe and the plasma collection cavity; and the negative pressure suction tube is connected between the water collecting cavity and the negative pressure suction device. When the negative pressure suction device is used for performing negative pressure suction on blood in the surgical wound of the body of a patient, plasma and water can be separated, so that medical staff can intuitively determine the bleeding amount of the surgical wound of the body of the patient according to the plasma amount; whether the patient is transfused or not in the operation process is determined according to the bleeding amount, the blood transfusion amount of the patient is determined when the patient needs to be transfused, the related operation is visual, clear and feasible, the operation is safe and reliable, and the application is high in practicability.

**（中文摘要）**

本实用新型公开血水分离负压吸引装置；包括导液管、排液管、积液收集器、负压吸引管及负压吸引器；积液收集器具有血浆收集腔及水收集腔；血浆收集腔底部开有连至水收集腔的导液口；导液口处安装有用以阻隔血浆且以使水通过的血浆分离膜；排液管连于导液管与血浆收集腔之间；负压吸引管连于水收集腔与负压吸引器之间。使用本申请对病人身体手术创口内血水进行负压吸引时，可实现血浆与水分离，使得医护人员能直观的根据血浆量确定病人身体手术创口部位出血量，进而根据出血量来确定手术过程中是否给病人输血及在需要给病人输血时确定给病人输血的输血量，相关操作直观明了，切实可行，使得手术安全可靠，进而，本申请实用性强。
