## 📝 摘要

The utility model relates to the technical field of hand and foot surgery, and discloses a surgical tourniquet for hand and foot surgery, in order to improve the flexible usability of the surgical tourniquet for hand and foot surgery, a hoop frame is in involution connection through a pin shaft, positioning sleeves are symmetrically arranged on the two sides of the hoop frame, compression springs are embedded and clamped in the inner sides of the positioning sleeves, and the compression springs are connected with the hoop frame through a pin shaft. And fastening pressing plates are symmetrically arranged on the end face, penetrating through the inner wall of the hoop frame, of the top end of the compression spring, and air cushions are symmetrically arranged on the end faces of the fastening pressing plates. According to the tourniquet, the two groups of hoop frames are used for hooping and positioning a blood loss part of a hand and a foot, and under the elastic folding and pressing of the fastening pressing plate and the air pressure extrusion of the air cushion, the tourniquet is convenient for medical personnel to flexibly operate and use, has double hemostasis performance, can be convenient for the medical personnel to adjust the tightness of the tourniquet, and is convenient to use. Therefore, a worker can intuitively adjust the tightening degree of the tourniquet according to the blood flow condition, and the hemostasis function of the tourniquet is enhanced.

**（中文摘要）**

本实用新型涉及手足外科技术领域，公开了一种手足外科用手术止血带，为了提高手足外科用手术止血带的灵活使用性，所述环箍架通过销轴对合连接，且环箍架的两侧对称设置有定位套筒，所述定位套筒的内侧嵌入卡合有压缩弹簧，所述压缩弹簧的顶端贯穿环箍架的内壁端面对称设置有紧固压板，所述紧固压板的端面对称设置有气垫。本实用新型通过两组环箍架对手足失血部位进行环箍定位，在紧固压板的弹性对合压固以及气垫的气压挤压下，其不仅便于医疗人员灵活操控使用，同时具有双重的止血性能，能够便于医疗人员对止血带的松紧程度进行调节，以便于工作人员根据血流情况直观的调节止血带的紧绷程度，加强止血带的止血功能。
