## 📝 摘要

The body is provided with a positioning ring and a handle, the extension piece is movably connected with the body and bends and extends towards the far end, the functional piece is arranged at the end of the extension piece, an operator can position the wrist in the positioning ring and hold the handle, so that the body is stressed to perturb relative to the extension piece, and the body is driven by the functional piece to move relative to the extension piece. Therefore, the operation position of the functional part is adjusted. Therefore, the bionic surgical instrument can provide intuitive control and better operation hand feeling, and the accuracy of instrument operation can be improved.

**（中文摘要）**

本申请提供一种仿生手术器械包括具有定位环和手柄的本体、活动连接本体并朝远端弯曲延伸的延伸件，设于延伸件的端部的功能件，其中，术者可将腕部定位于定位环中并握持手柄，以使本体受力相对于延伸件挠动，以调节功能件的操作位置。据此，本申请的仿生手术器械可提供直觉式控制以及较佳地操作手感，可提高器械操作的准确性。
