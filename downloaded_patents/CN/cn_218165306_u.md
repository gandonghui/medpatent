## 📝 摘要

The positioning device with the traction mechanism for the upper limb surgery comprises a fixing plate, and the traction mechanism used for retracting a surgical incision is further arranged on the fixing plate. The traction mechanism comprises a belt positioned on the fixing plate, and a hook is arranged on the belt. After the traction mechanism is arranged, the hook can be hooked on a surgical incision to retract the surgical incision and fully expose the incision when operations such as vascular nerve operations are carried out, so that a doctor can conveniently and intuitively carry out the operations.

**（中文摘要）**

具有牵引机构的上肢手术用定位装置，包括固定板，所述的固定板上还设有用于牵开手术切口的牵引机构。所述的牵引机构包括定位在固定板上的带子，所述的带子上设有钩子。本实用新型设置牵引机构后，在进行血管神经术等手术时，可将钩子钩在手术切口上，将手术切口牵开，充分暴露切口，便于医生直观手术。
