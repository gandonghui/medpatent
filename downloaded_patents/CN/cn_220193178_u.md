## 📝 摘要

The utility model discloses a novel remote medical device, which relates to the technical field of medical equipment, and aims to solve the problems that the existing remote medical device cannot directly and intuitively guide the surgical site of a patient and the practicability needs to be improved, and adopts the technical scheme that the novel remote medical device comprises a box body, and a mounting hole is formed in the upper end surface of the box body; a first telescopic supporting rod is fixedly mounted in the mounting hole, a second telescopic supporting rod is fixedly mounted at the upper end of the first telescopic supporting rod, one end of the second telescopic supporting rod is fixedly connected with a guiding mechanism, the guiding mechanism comprises a first mounting seat, and a through hole is formed in the upper surface of the first mounting seat; and a camera is fixedly mounted in the through hole, and a first motor is fixedly mounted at one end of the first mounting seat. The effects that the surgical site of the patient can be visually and conveniently guided, and practicability is high are achieved.

**（中文摘要）**

本实用新型公开了一种新型远程医疗装置，其涉及医疗设备技术领域，旨在解决现有的远程医疗装置不能直接直观的在患者的手术部位上进行指导，实用性有待提高的问题，其技术方案要点包括箱体，所述箱体的上端面上设置有安装孔，且安装孔的内部固定安装有第一伸缩支撑杆，所述第一伸缩支撑杆的上端固定安装有第二伸缩支撑杆，所述第二伸缩支撑杆的一端固定连接有引导机构，所述引导机构包含有第一安装座，所述第一安装座的上表面上设置有通孔，且通孔的内部固定安装有摄像头，所述第一安装座的一端固定安装有第一电机。达到了能直观便捷的对患者的手术部位进行指导，实用性强的效果。
