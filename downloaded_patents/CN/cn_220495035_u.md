## 📝 摘要

The bionic surgical instrument comprises a handle, an extension part extending from the handle and capable of perturbing relative to the handle, and a functional part arranged at the head end of the extension part, when an operator holds the handle, the extension part is located below the wrist of the operator relative to the perturbing center of the handle, the handle can be stressed to perturb relative to the extension part, and the functional part is arranged at the head end of the extension part. The bending posture of the head end of the extension part is adjusted, so that the operation position of the functional part is adjusted. Accordingly, the bionic surgical instrument can provide intuitive control, and accords with ergonomic design, so that the control accuracy of the functional part is improved.

**（中文摘要）**

本申请提供一种仿生手术器械包括手柄、自手柄延伸并可相对于手柄挠动的延伸件、设于延伸件的头端的功能件，其中，当术者握持于手柄上时，延伸件相对于手柄的挠动中心位于术者腕部的下方，手柄可受力相对于延伸件挠动，以调节延伸件的头端的弯曲姿态，借以调整功能件的操作位置。据此，本申请的仿生手术器械可提供直觉式控制，并符合人体工学设计，以提高功能件的控制精准度。
