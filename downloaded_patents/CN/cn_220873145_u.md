## 📝 摘要

The utility model relates to a knotting training device with a self-checking function. The knotting training device comprises a knotting training device body and a self-checking circuit. The knotting training device comprises a base, a support, a hollow latex tourniquet, a metal weight and a magnet piece, the support and the magnet piece are arranged on the base, the hollow latex tourniquet is arranged on the support, insulating plugs are arranged at the two ends of the hollow latex tourniquet, sensitive conductive liquid is arranged in the hollow latex tourniquet, and a hook is arranged at the bottom of the hollow latex tourniquet. The metal weight is connected with the bottom end of the hook and is magnetically connected with the magnet sheet; the self-checking circuit comprises a power supply, a main switch, a first indicating lamp and a second indicating lamp. The first indicator lamp and the hollow latex tourniquet form a first detection circuit; the second indicating lamp, the metal weight and the magnet sheet form a second detection circuit. According to the utility model, the knotting effect, the stability of the knotting technique and the knotting strength can be automatically detected in the knotting process, the training effect is intuitively reflected, two training modes of common knotting and deep knotting are provided, and the knotting environment under a real surgical operation is more comprehensively simulated.

**（中文摘要）**

本申请涉及一种具有自检功能的打结训练装置，包括打结训练装置和自检电路；打结训练装置包括底座、支架、中空乳胶压脉带、金属秤砣和磁铁片，支架和磁铁片设置在底座上，中空乳胶压脉带设置在支架上，两端设置有绝缘塞，内部设置有敏感导电液，底部设置有挂钩；金属秤砣与挂钩底端连接并与磁铁片通过磁力连接；自检电路包括电源、总开关、第一指示灯和第二指示灯；第一指示灯和中空乳胶压脉带构成第一检测电路；第二指示灯、金属秤砣和磁铁片构成第二检测电路。本实用新型能够在打结过程中自动检测打结效果、打结手法的稳定性以及打结力度，直观反应训练效果，并具有普通打结和深部打结两种训练模式，更全面地模拟真实外科手术下的打结环境。
