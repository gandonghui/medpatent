## 📝 摘要

The utility model belongs to the technical field of medical equipment, and particularly relates to a thoracic surgery lung cancer operation teaching device which comprises an operation table, a cavity is formed in the operation table, a guide rail is arranged in the cavity, a placement groove is formed in the operation table, and an auxiliary plate is slidably installed at the bottom end in the placement groove; the teaching device further comprises a teaching assembly, the teaching assembly is rotationally arranged above the operation table, the teaching assembly comprises a display screen rotationally connected with the operation table, and a connecting block is fixedly arranged on one side of the display screen; the adjusting device comprises a driving motor and an electric push rod which are fixedly arranged in the operation table, the output end of the driving motor is fixedly connected with the connecting block, and the output end of the electric push rod is fixedly connected with the auxiliary plate; and the lung model is fixedly arranged on the auxiliary plate. According to the utility model, through the demonstration assembly and the lung model, the practical conditions of the lung structure and the surgical operation can be more intuitively known.

**（中文摘要）**

本实用属于医疗设备技术领域，具体涉及一种胸外科肺癌手术示教装置，包括操作台，所述操作台内开设有腔体，所述腔体内开设有导轨，且所述操作台上开设有放置槽，所述放置槽内部底端滑动安装有辅助板；还包括示教组件，所述示教组件转动设于所述操作台上方，所述示教组件包括与所述操作台转动连接的显示屏，且所述显示屏一侧固定设有连接块；调节装置，所述调节装置包括固定设于所述操作台内的驱动电机以及电动推杆，所述驱动电机输出端与所述连接块固定连接，且所述电动推杆输出端与所述辅助板固定连接；肺部模型，所述肺部模型固定设于所述辅助板上。本实用通过示教组件以及肺部模型可以更直观地了解肺部结构和手术操作的实际情况。
