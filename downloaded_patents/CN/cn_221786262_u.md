## 📝 摘要

The utility model discloses a magnetic compatible radiography guide wire for interventional therapy in MRI (Magnetic Resonance Imaging) equipment. The magnetic compatible radiography guide wire comprises a core wire, a water-based polymer coating layer coated outside the core wire, and a cylindrical elastic piece arranged between the core wire and the water-based polymer coating layer, the core wire is made of metal capable of cutting magnetic lines; a hydrophilic coating is arranged on the surface of the water-based polymer coating layer; the hydrophilic coating is sprayed on the water-based polymer coating layer through a plasma spraying process; ionizing radiation does not exist, MR perspective (real-time guided operation) can be conducted, artifacts of the puncture needle do not interfere with display of a target area, three-dimensional image reconstruction can be achieved, the relation between the target area and normal tissue can be clearly displayed, and damage to nerves and blood vessels can be avoided to the maximum extent through MR guiding. The MR image can display the interventional diagnosis and treatment image almost in real time; therefore, a surgeon can know the position relation between a surgical instrument and a lesion and an important tissue structure at any time, so that the complicated operation is simpler, more convenient and more intuitive.

**（中文摘要）**

本实用新型公开了一种在MRI设备中介入治疗用磁兼容造影导丝，包括芯丝、包覆于芯丝外部的水性聚合物包覆层，以及设于芯丝与所述水性聚合物包覆层之间的筒状弹性件；芯丝由能够切割磁力线的金属制成；水性聚合物包覆层表面设有亲水涂层；亲水涂层通过等离子喷涂工艺喷涂于水性聚合物包覆层上；无电离辐射，可作MR透视(实时导引手术)，穿刺针伪影对靶向区域显示无干扰，可三维图像重建，能清楚显示靶向区域与正常组织关系，MR引导可以最大程度避免神经和血管损伤；使MR图像达到近乎实时显示介入诊疗影像；使手术医生就能够随时了解手术器械与病变、重要组织结构的位置关系，从而使复杂的操作更加简捷、直观。
