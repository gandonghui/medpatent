## 📝 摘要

The utility model relates to the technical field of medical instruments, and provides a main end operating table of an interventional surgical robot, which comprises an operating table body, a display component, a translation sliding handle and a push rotating handle, the display assembly, the translation sliding handle and the push rotating handle are arranged on the operation table body. The interventional surgical robot adjusts the action according to the linear displacement of the translation sliding handle and the angular displacement of the pushing rotating handle; the linear displacement resistance of the translation sliding handle and/or the angular displacement resistance of the pushing rotating handle are/is in positive correlation with the action resistance of the interventional operation machine. By arranging components such as the display and the handle, on one hand, linear displacement and angular displacement of the handle are respectively associated with action resistance of the interventional operation robot, the perceptual resistance in the operation process is improved, the operation safety is improved, on the other hand, related states and information in the operation can be synchronously displayed, and the operation safety is improved. And a doctor can observe the whole process of the operation more intuitively.

**（中文摘要）**

本实用新型涉及医疗器械技术领域，本实用新型提供一种介入手术机器人的主端操作台，包括：操作台本体、显示组件、平移滑动手柄和推送旋转手柄；显示组件、平移滑动手柄和推送旋转手柄分别设置于操作台本体；介入手术机器人根据平移滑动手柄的线位移和推送旋转手柄的角位移调节动作；平移滑动手柄的线位移阻力和/或推送旋转手柄的角位移阻力与介入手术机器的动作阻力正相关。本实用新型通过设置显示和手柄等部件，一方面将手柄的线位移和角位移分别与介入手术机器人的动作阻力进行关联，提升了手术过程中感知遇到阻力的大小，提高手术安全性，另一方面能够同步显示手术中的相关状态和信息，便于医生更直观的观察手术的整体进程。
