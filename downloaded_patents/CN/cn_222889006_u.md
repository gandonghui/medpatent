## 📝 摘要

The utility model discloses a navigation device for implanting and locking an intramedullary nail, which relates to the technical field of surgical instruments and comprises a holder with one end used for being connected with the intramedullary nail provided with a far-end lock hole and the other end connected with an intramedullary nail tracer through a support; the support is connected with the far-end lockhole guider through the abduction adjusting assembly, the anterior arch adjusting assembly and the axial adjusting assembly in sequence, and the far-end lockhole guider is further connected with a far-end lockhole tracer; the navigation device can help a doctor to intuitively observe the real-time position of the main nail of the intramedullary nail in the long marrow cavity of the human body and the position of a far-end lockhole in an operation, so that the intramedullary nail can be conveniently implanted into an ideal position, and the operation efficiency is improved.

**（中文摘要）**

本实用新型公开了一种用于髓内钉植入及锁钉的导航装置，涉及手术器械技术领域，包括：把持器，一端用于连接髓内钉，髓内钉上设置有远端锁孔，把持器的另一端通过支架与髓内钉示踪器；远端锁孔导向器，支架依次通过外展调节组件、前弓调节组件和轴向调节组件与远端锁孔导向器连接，远端锁孔导向器上还连接有远端锁孔示踪器；该导航装置能够帮助医生在手术中，直观观察髓内钉主钉在人体长骨髓腔中实时位置和远端锁孔的位置，方便将髓内钉植入到理想位置，提高手术效率。
