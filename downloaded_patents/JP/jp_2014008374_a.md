## 📝 摘要

PROBLEM TO BE SOLVED: To provide a surgical operation support device, capable of intuitively operating a treatment part even when a surgical operation instrument changes in projection length and rotation angle.SOLUTION: A surgical operation support device includes: an operation input part for generating an operation command based on the input from a user; an operation part where a channel 50 for penetrating a surgical operation instrument 200 provided with a treatment part at the tip part of a soft surgical operation insertion part 210 is formed for moving the channel; travel detecting means 45 for detecting the travel of the treatment part which is at least one of a projection length L1 how the treatment part of the surgical operation instrument penetrating the channel projects forward from a tip opening 68a of the channel and the rotation angle of the surgical operation instrument around the axis C1 of the channel; operation part information calculating means for calculating operation part information which can specify the position and attitude of the operation part at the tip opening; and an operation control part for controlling the operation of the operation part based on an operation command.
