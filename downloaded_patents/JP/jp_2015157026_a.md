## 📝 摘要

PROBLEM TO BE SOLVED: To intuitively match an operation input unit to be operated with a treatment instrument driven by a slave, without performing troublesome setting operation or switching operation.SOLUTION: A manipulator system for surgical operation comprises: a manipulator 11 comprising a treatment instrument 4 including at least one joint and a drive unit 5 that is attached to the treatment instrument to drive the treatment instrument; an operation input unit 10 to which operation input for making the treatment instrument 4 perform desired operation by drive of the drive unit 5 of the manipulator 11 is performed; and a control unit 12 for controlling the drive unit 5 on the basis of the operation input to the operation input unit 10. The treatment instrument 4 comprises an identification information holding unit 6 for holding identification information on the treatment instrument 4. The operation input unit 10 comprises an operation side reading unit 9 for reading out the identification information on the treatment instrument 4 by performing short-distance communication with the identification information holding unit 6 of the treatment instrument 4. The control unit 12 controls the drive unit 5 on the basis of the identification information on the treatment instrument 4 read out by the operation side reading unit 9.
