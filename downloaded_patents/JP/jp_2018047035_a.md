## 📝 摘要

PROBLEM TO BE SOLVED: To provide an inexpensive medical support device that enables preparations prior to an operation to be made in a short time, can be carried to an operation room, a ward, etc., and enables a three-dimensional position, etc. of a lesion to be displayed by a realistic image that can be intuitively understood by a surgeon, etc. easily in a surgical operation, and to provide a medical support method.SOLUTION: A medical support method includes: acquisition of three-dimensional medical information on a subject; acquisition of three-dimensional texture information on the subject; acquisition of three-dimensional appearance feature information on the subject; and mapping of the three-dimensional medical information and the three-dimensional texture information to the three-dimensional appearance feature information; and displaying, in an overlapped manner, the three-dimensional medical information and the three-dimensional texture information that have been mapped.SELECTED DRAWING: Figure 3
