## 📝 摘要

PROBLEM TO BE SOLVED: To provide a surgery assist device capable of controlling an attitude of a mechanically-controlled surgical instrument using a surgical instrument for treatment, while suppressing influence of intuitive and measurement errors.SOLUTION: The surgery assist device for controlling an attitude of a surgical instrument inserted from a first hole of an abdominal wall into a body cavity and mechanically driven, includes: measurement means that measures an insertion depth and insertion angle of a member inserted from a second hole of the abdominal wall into the body cavity; a joint part whose position in the cavity is specified by the insertion depth and insertion angle of the surgical instrument; and calculation means that, on the surgical instrument including a portion being movable at one or more degree of freedom by the joint part, determines a target position of a control object point for controlling mobility of the portion with a position of the joint part defined as a reference, on the basis of a measurement result by the measurement means. The calculation means determines the target position of the control object point according to variations in the insertion angle and insertion depth of the member from a first reference time when the control of the mobility of the portion with the point of the joint part defined as the reference, is started.SELECTED DRAWING: Figure 3
