## 📝 摘要

To provide a system and method for operating a foot pedal assembly, which allow for appropriate, reliable and intuitive operation of a surgical instrument.SOLUTION: A system 20 has a safety lock 21. The safety lock is configured to at least partially prevent use of a surgical instrument 22. A foot switch assembly 1 is operatively configured to control the surgical instrument and has a plurality of switches. There are a memory 26 for storing a predetermined switch sequence associated with the switches of the foot switch assembly, and a control unit 25 configured to determine whether a sequence of input switch actuations from the foot switch assembly corresponds to the predetermined switch sequence, and if so, to deactivate the safety lock in response to the sequence of input switch actuations corresponding to the predetermined switch sequence.SELECTED DRAWING: Figure 5
