## 📝 摘要

To provide: an end tool having a plurality of degrees of freedom; a manipulation part configured to intuitively control the operation of the end tool; and a power transmission part configured to transmit driving force of the manipulation part to the end tool for operating the end tool according to manipulations of the manipulation part.SOLUTION: The present invention relates to an instrument 100 for surgery and, more specifically, to an instrument for surgery which can be manually operated in order to be used for laparoscopic surgery or various types of surgery.The surgical instrument includes a first jaw and a second jaw formed to be freely rotatable. The operation part 110 controls rotation of an end tool 120 in two or more directions, the end tool 120 being formed so as to be freely rotatable in the two or more directions. The operation part includes: a yaw operation part 112 that controls a yaw motion of the end tool; and an actuation operation part 113 that is formed on one side of the yaw operation part and controls an actuation motion of the end tool.SELECTED DRAWING: Figure 2
