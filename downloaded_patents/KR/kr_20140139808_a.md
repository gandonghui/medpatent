## 📝 摘要

Disclosed is a flexure-type surgical apparatus which can easily access a flexuous affected area and can operate, can let a plurality of surgical tools enter the flexuous affected area at the same time, and can be more easily and conveniently controlled by an operator. The flexure-type surgical apparatus comprises a flexure-type frame having at least two channels; and at least two surgical tools inserted into the flexure-type frame through at least two channels so that the operator can respectively hold the tools with one hand and control the same, matching an operation direction of the operator and a rotation direction of an upper end and enabling intuitive surgery.
