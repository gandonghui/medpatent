## 📝 摘要

An operation system capable of attaching/detaching a commercial surgical apparatus, according to one embodiment, comprises: a knob rotation device capable of being attached to /detached from a control handle; a surgical tool drive device including a rotating support and detachably attached to a surgical tool; a surgical instrument adjustment device; an endoscope controller; a surgical tool controller; and a control unit. It is an object of one embodiment to provide the surgical apparatus which can be used by being detachably attached to a commercial surgical apparatus to intuitively change the controller of the commercial surgical apparatus to a user.
