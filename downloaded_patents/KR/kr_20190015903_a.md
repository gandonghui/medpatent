## 📝 摘要

The present invention relates to a surgical recording and relay system using a three-dimensional camera and a head mount display. The system can intuitively change and observe in real time a whole image of an operating room and a close-up image of an affected part. In addition, the system comprises: a plurality of cameras installed in the operating room; a recording and relay computing device for receiving a plurality of image data from the plurality of cameras; and a head mount display which receives and displays at least one of the plurality of image data based on position information of the system and is worn by a medical staff.
