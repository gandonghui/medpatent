## 📝 摘要

Provided are a surgical robot, a surgical robot system and a handheld medical apparatus, capable of sufficiently securing a driving range of a surgical apparatus (for example, an ablation loop), and allowing a surgeon to intuitively operate a master device. The surgical robot comprises: a base; a robot arm disposed on the base and performing linear and rotational driving; a controller and a resectoscope disposed on the robot arm; an endoscope disposed in the resectoscope and performing rotational and bending driving by the controller; and an ablation loop disposed in the resectoscope and performing withdrawal driving by the controller.
