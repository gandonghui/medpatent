## 📝 摘要

According to the present invention, provided is a surgical system using a surgical robot which comprises: an imaging unit for generating a surgical image; a master console unit in which a manipulation unit capable of three-dimensional manipulation is installed; a robot surgical procedure unit driven by receiving an electrical signal from the master console unit according to movement of the manipulation unit; and a display unit made of a transparent material and disposed between the master console unit and the robot surgical procedure unit, wherein information related to surgery is displayed on the display unit, thereby allowing a user to intuitively check a patient and surrounding conditions during surgery to increase efficiency and accuracy of the surgery.
