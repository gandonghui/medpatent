## 📝 摘要

A surgical navigation system based on a C-arm interface related to one aspect of the present invention, comprises: a surgical planning portion for establishing a three-dimensional surgical plan information for treating a fracture site of a patient by using CT images and MR images; a C-arm interface portion for receiving the three-dimensional surgical plan information; an image matching portion for mutually matching points of the patient's skeleton included in the three-dimensional surgical plan information with points related to the skeleton detected in images acquired by the C-arm, and for overlaying the two-dimensional geometry of a fixation device utilized in the surgery included in the three-dimensional surgical plan information onto the mutually matched images to produce a matched image; and a display portion for receiving and outputting the matched image, and further outputting navigation information related to the insertion of the fixation device and placement of the same within the patient's body in real time. In the present invention, a treatment plan in the form of a three-dimensional image for fracture surgery can be generated according to the angle of the C-arm, and if the C-arm image is matched to the C-arm image, the conquest plan contained in the three-dimensional image and the placement and fixation plan of the internal fixation device can be overlaid on the C-arm image as it is to show an intuitive surgical plan to the surgeon.
