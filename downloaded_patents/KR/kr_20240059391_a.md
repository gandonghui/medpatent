## 📝 摘要

An object of the present invention is to provide a laparoscopic surgical device which enables a user to easily use the laparoscopic surgical device as a handle moving direction of a body moving by a hand movement of the user is the same as a moving direction of a moving member of a front end of an extension rod connected from the body, and vertical and horizontal movements are smoothly connected to enable intuitive operation. The laparoscopic surgery device having an intuitive operation function comprises: a body having a handle; a first support member having an extension piece rotating around a first hinge shaft and a second hinge shaft; a second support member rotating around a third hinge shaft and having a disc member on an upper end thereof; an extension rod rotatably coupled to the disc member of the second support member; a moving member moving in one direction due to the action of an external force; and a plurality of wires respectively connected from a plurality of points of a front end of the moving member to a plurality of points of the extension piece of the first support member.
