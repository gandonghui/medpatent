## 📝 摘要

Disclosed is an endoscope assembly (13) applied to a medical water-jet scalpel (1), the endoscope assembly comprising a sheath catheter (131), a camera (132) and at least one LED lamp (133), wherein the sheath catheter (131) comprises a first end (1311) and a second end (1312) which are arranged in sequence; the first end (1311) is provided with a first accommodation space (1313) and at least one second accommodation space (1314), the at least one second accommodation space (1314) is located at one side of the first accommodation space (1313), the camera (132) is arranged in the first accommodation space (1313), and the at least one LED lamp (133) is correspondingly arranged in the at least one second accommodation space (1314). By means of the endoscope assembly (13), doctors and observers can intuitively observe a surgical site of a patient, so that the surgical condition can be observed more conveniently, and the surgical safety is improved.

**（中文摘要）**

一种应用于医用水刀（1）的内窥镜组件（13），其包括鞘管（131）、摄像头（132）及至少一LED灯（133），鞘管（131）包括依序设置的第一端（1311）与第二端（1312），第一端（1311）具有第一容置空间（1313）及至少一第二容置空间（1314），至少一第二容置空间（1314）位于第一容置空间（1313）的一侧，摄像头（132）设置于第一容置空间（1313），至少一LED灯（133）对应设置于至少一第二容置空间（1314）。该内窥镜组件（13）能够使医生和观看者直观地观察患者手术部位，使之更加方便地观察手术情况，增加了手术的安全性。
