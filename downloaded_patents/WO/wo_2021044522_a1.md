## 📝 摘要

[Problem] To enable assistance of effective manipulation of a surgical instrument. [Solution] Provided is a surgery assist device including: an area information acquisition unit that acquires area information including three-dimensional information of the processing target area; an instrument information acquisition unit that acquires instrument information including three-dimensional information of an instrument; a condition information computing unit that computes condition information for the processing target area of the instrument according to the area information and the instrument information; and a presentation unit that presents the computed condition information. The surgery assist system according to this embodiment may display an image of an operative field superimposed with the present angle and a suitable angle or the like of a surgical instrument 212. So an operator 5 may rapidly and intuitively understand the condition of the surgical instrument 212 in the operative field.
