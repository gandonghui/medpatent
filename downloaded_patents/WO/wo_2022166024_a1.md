## 📝 摘要

A method and device for planning the initial poses of surgical arms of a laparoscopic surgical robot. The method comprises: obtaining a set minimally invasive incision position and surgical area position (S1); obtaining multiple initial surgical arm joint position schemes according to the minimally invasive incision position and the surgical area position (S2); and separately calculating a performance index of each initial surgical arm joint position scheme according to the surgical area position (S3). In the method, the multiple initial surgical arm joint position schemes are obtained by means of the minimally invasive incision position and the surgical area position, and the performance index of each initial surgical arm joint position scheme is calculated. Medical personnel can intuitively analyze the advantages and disadvantages of the multiple initial surgical arm joint position schemes by observing the performance index. Therefore, on one hand, the method can reduce the workload of the medical personnel, and on the other hand, the preoperative preparation work of a surgical robot assisted minimally invasive surgery can be quickly completed, so as to increase the setting speed of the medical personnel for the initial poses of surgical arms.

在本实施例中，在手术过程中，为更好地保证手术进程的进展，需要保证左、右微器械在手术区域内有足够的合作空间，一般需要左、右微器械在手术区域内的合作空间达到最大。由此，医护人员可以选择微器械体内协作空间最大时对应的手术臂关节的初始位置方案，从而可以保证在左、右手术臂在手术区域位置的无碰撞工作空间的基础上，左、右微器械在手术区域位置内的合作空间达到最大。
