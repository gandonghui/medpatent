## 📝 摘要

The present invention relates to an end tool of a surgical instrument and an electric cauterization surgical instrument comprising same. Specifically, the present invention relates to, in a surgical instrument which is mounted on a robotic arm or is manually operable for use in laparoscopic surgery or various other types of surgery, an end tool of a surgical instrument comprising an end tool, and an electric cauterization surgical instrument comprising same, wherein the end tool can be rotated in two or more directions and operates to intuitively match the operation of a manipulation part.
