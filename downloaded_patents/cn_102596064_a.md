# Patent: CN-102596064-A

## Title
Curved cannula and robotic manipulator

## Abstract
A robotic surgical system is configured with rigid, curved cannula (416 a) that extend through the same opening into the patient. A surgical instrument (500) having passive flexible shaft (506) extends through the curved cannulas. These cannulas are oriented to guide instruments toward the surgical site. A teleoperational control system is disclosed that moves the curved cannulas and their associated instruments in a manner that allows the surgeon to experience intuitive control. The flexible shaft instruments are controlled as if extending along a virtual straight insertion and extraction axis. Various port members (1402) are disclosed that support a curved cannula within the single opening. Cannula support fixture (1902) that support the cannulas during insertion into the single opening and mounting onto a robotic manipulator are disclosed.
