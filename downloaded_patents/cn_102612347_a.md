# Patent: CN-102612347-A

## Title
弯曲套管、机器人操纵器和手术端口

## Abstract
A robotic surgical system is configured with rigid, curved cannula (416 a) that extend through the same opening into the patient. A surgical instrument (500) having passive flexible shaft (506) extends through the curved cannulas. These cannulas are oriented to guide instruments toward the surgical site. Various port members (1402) are disclosed that support a curved cannula within the single opening. In one aspect, the surgical instrument channel extend at different angles between the top and bottom surfaces of the port member. In another aspect, the funnel portion is separated from the tongue portion by a waist, and the surgical instrument channel extends from the funnel portion through the waist. Cannula support fixture (1902) that support the cannulas during insertion into the single opening and mounting onto a robotic manipulator are disclosed. A teleoperational control system is disclosed that moves the curved cannulas and their associated instruments in a manner that allows the surgeon to experience intuitive control.
