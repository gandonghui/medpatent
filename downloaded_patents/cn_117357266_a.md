# Patent: CN-117357266-A

## Title
Flexible endoscope operation robot system

## Abstract
A flexible endoscopic surgical robotic system includes a flexible endoscope configured with a drive module configured to provide an endoscope insertion portion axial motion, an endoscope insertion portion rotation, and an endoscope distal bending degree of freedom, and a surgical instrument having a drive end coupled to the drive module of the endoscope to enable the surgical instrument to move axially and rotate with the endoscope. The driving end of the surgical instrument is coupled with the driving module of the endoscope, so that the surgical instrument can axially move and rotate along with the endoscope, extra manual operation is omitted, and the surgical instrument is convenient and simple and can intuitively realize remote operation; the invention also has the advantages of high integration level, small volume, convenient adaptation and disassembly of device parts by adopting a modularized design, and high accuracy and reliability by adopting a rigid-flexible balance and flexible parallel wrist joint structure with high flexibility; meanwhile, the invention has strong compatibility with the existing flexible endoscope system and good application prospect.
