# Patent: CN-102596062-B

## Title
弯曲套管、机器人操纵器和具有被动柔性轴的手术器械

## Abstract
A robotic surgical system is configured with rigid, curved cannula(s) (416 a) that extend through the same opening into the patient. A surgical instrument (500) having passive flexible shaft(s) (506) extends through the curved cannulas. A force isolation element (856) within the flexible shaft prevents bending of the shaft from affecting an end effector actuation element extending through the shaft. These cannulas are oriented to guide instruments toward the surgical site. Various port members (1402) are disclosed that support a curved cannula within the single opening. Cannula support fixture(s) (1902) that support the cannulas during insertion into the single opening and mounting onto a robotic manipulator are disclosed. A teleoperational control system is disclosed that moves the curved cannulas and their associated instruments in a manner that allows the surgeon to experience intuitive control.
