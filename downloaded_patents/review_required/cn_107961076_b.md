# Patent: CN-107961076-B

## Title
User initiated breakaway clutching of surgical mounting platform

## Abstract
This document relates to user-initiated breakaway clutching of a surgical mounting platform. Robotic and/or surgical devices, systems, and methods include kinematic linkage structures and associated control systems configured to facilitate preparation of the systems for use. One or more kinematic linkage subsystems may include joints that are actively driven, passive, or a mixture of both. The set-up mode utilizes an intuitive user interface in which one or more joints are initially held stationary by a brake or joint driver system. The user may articulate the joint by manually pushing the linkage with a force, torque, etc., that exceeds a manual articulation threshold. Articulation of the mobile joint is facilitated by varying the signal transmitted to the brake or driver system. The system may sense completion of reconfiguration from the joint velocity being below a threshold, optionally for a desired dwell time.
