# Patent: CN-119184790-A

## Title
Bone-fixed robotic-assisted surgery system for knee replacement

## Abstract
Systems, methods, and devices for a bone-mounted robotic-assisted orthopaedic surgical system are described. The system enables accurate positioning of implants, soft tissue balancing and tool navigation during surgery, particularly during partial or total knee replacement surgery. The system employs a bone-mounted robotic arm with an end effector to achieve precise surgical tool positioning, implant placement, and soft tissue balancing. The configurable robotic system requires little training of the surgeon, is intuitive to operate, is similar to traditional instrument surgery, and occupies a small area. The system can be matched with the traditional instrument, the patient-specific instrument, the sensor auxiliary system and the computer auxiliary system, the operation time is not required to be increased, and safer and more accurate operation can be realized by means of the robot auxiliary system and the technology thereof.
