# Patent: WO-2023287253-A1

## Title
수술용 인스트루먼트의 엔드 툴 및 이를 구비한 수술용 인스트루먼트

## Abstract
The present invention relates to an end tool of a surgical instrument, and a surgical instrument comprising same. Specifically, the present invention relates to: an end tool of a surgical instrument mounted to a robotic arm or manually operable for use in laparoscopy or various types of surgery, the surgical instrument being equipped with an end tool which can be rotated in two or more directions and is operated to intuitively match the operation of a manipulation part; and a surgical instrument comprising same.
